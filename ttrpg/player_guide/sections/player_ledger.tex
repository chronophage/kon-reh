\chapter{Character Sheet}
łabel{ch:charsheet}

Use this character sheet to record your hero’s story. Fill it in pencil – debts, wounds, and Obligation change often. The layout follows the order of character creation: start with Attributes and Skills, then add Talents and magic, then track your resources.

\begin{tcolorbox}[

    colback=gray!5, colframe=black!50, 
    title={łarge\bfseries Quick Reference (inside cover)}, 
    fonttitle=\bfseries,
    breakable
]
\small
\textbf{Core Dice:} Attribute + Skill d10s. Success on 6+. 1 = Story Beat for GM.\\
\textbf{Difficulty (DV):} 2 routine, 3 default, 4 hard, 5+ extreme.\\
\textbf{Position:} Dominant (re‑roll failure), Controlled (normal), Desperate (re‑roll success).\\
\textbf{Outcomes:} Clean (S$≥$DV, no SB), Success with SB (S$≥$DV, SB$>$0), Partial (0$<$S$<$DV, gain 1 Boon), Miss (S=0, gain 2 Boons).\\
\textbf{Boons:} Re‑roll a die, improve Position, activate Asset, convert 2 Boons $→$ 1 XP (once/session).\\
\textbf{Fatigue:} Each point worsens Position; fill track $→$ Harm+1, clear Fatigue.\\
\textbf{Harm:} 1 (–1 die), 2 (–2 dice), 3 (incapacitated).\\
\textbf{Obligation:} Capacity = Spirit+Presence. Over capacity $→$ 1 Fatigue/segment. Double over $→$ Patron intrusion.\\
\textbf{Corruption:} Timer size = Spirit. Fill $→$ Bloom (gain boon+burden), reset to Tier.
\end{tcolorbox}
