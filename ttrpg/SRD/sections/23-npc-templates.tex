\section{NPC Templates}

This section provides ready-to-use templates for non-player characters, from common guards to supernatural spirits. Each template includes a suggested \textbf{Cap} (capability tier), \textbf{Harm} output, typical dice pools, and special abilities. GMs may adjust values up or down by one step to suit the fiction.

\subsection{Template Format}

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Name:} Type of NPC.
  \item \textbf{Cap:} 1–5 (training and threat level).
  \item \textbf{Harm:} Typical damage output on a successful hit.
  \item \textbf{Dice Pools:} Suggested Attribute + Skill combinations.
  \item \textbf{Armor:} Light / Medium / Heavy / None.
  \item \textbf{Special:} Unique abilities or tags.
  \item \textbf{Leash (for spirits):} Cap + Spirit segments.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Humanoid NPCs}

\subsubsection{Bandit (Tier I)}
\vspace{0.2em}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Cap:} 1 & \textbf{Harm:} 1 (club or short sword) \\
\textbf{Dice:} Melee 2d (Body 2 + Melee 0), Ranged 2d (Wits 2 + Ranged 0) \\
\textbf{Armor:} None (leather scraps, no conversion) \\
\textbf{Special:} \textit{Cowardly} — when outnumbered or after taking Harm, must test Spirit (DV 3) or flee.
\end{tabular}

\subsubsection{Guard (Tier I–II)}
\vspace{0.2em}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Cap:} 2 & \textbf{Harm:} 2 (spear or short sword) \\
\textbf{Dice:} Melee 4d (Body 3 + Melee 1), Athletics 4d (Body 3 + Athletics 1) \\
\textbf{Armor:} Light (leather) — Harm 1 $\rightarrow$ Fatigue 1. \\
\textbf{Special:} \textit{Patrol Training} — +1 die to Notice when searching an area.
\end{tabular}

\subsubsection{Knight (Tier II–III)}
\vspace{0.2em}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Cap:} 3 & \textbf{Harm:} 3 (longsword or lance) \\
\textbf{Dice:} Melee 6d (Body 3 + Melee 3), Command 5d (Presence 3 + Command 2) \\
\textbf{Armor:} Heavy (plate) — Harm 3 $\rightarrow$ Fatigue 2. \\
\textbf{Special:} \textit{Challenge} — once per scene, force an enemy to engage in melee (Resist with Spirit, DV 4). \textit{Mounted} — +2 dice when charging from Far to Near.
\end{tabular}

\subsubsection{Cultist (Tier I–II)}
\vspace{0.2em}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Cap:} 1–2 & \textbf{Harm:} 1 (ritual dagger) \\
\textbf{Dice:} Arcana 4d (Wits 3 + Arcana 1), Deception 4d (Presence 3 + Deception 1) \\
\textbf{Armor:} None (robes). \\
\textbf{Special:} \textit{Ritual Chant} — when three or more cultists act together, they may perform a Low Rite as a group action (roll once with +2 dice). \textit{Fanatic} — immune to fear effects from their Patron's domain.
\end{tabular}

\subsubsection{Bandit Leader (Tier II)}
\vspace{0.2em}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Cap:} 3 & \textbf{Harm:} 2 (battleaxe) or 3 (critical) \\
\textbf{Dice:} Melee 6d (Body 3 + Melee 3), Command 5d (Presence 3 + Command 2) \\
\textbf{Armor:} Medium (chain) — Harm 2 $\rightarrow$ Fatigue 1. \\
\textbf{Special:} \textit{Rally} — once per scene, restore 1 Morale to a nearby bandit. \textit{Dirty Fighting} — may spend 1 Boon to impose –1 die on an enemy's next defense.
\end{tabular}

\subsection{Supernatural NPCs}

\subsubsection{Lesser Spirit (e.g., Shade, Emberling)}
\vspace{0.2em}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Cap:} 1 & \textbf{Harm:} 1 (chill touch or fiery nip) \\
\textbf{Dice:} Stealth 5d (Wits 3 + Stealth 2), Arcana 4d (Spirit 3 + Arcana 1) \\
\textbf{Armor:} None (incorporeal — half physical Harm, rounded down) \\
\textbf{Leash:} Cap + Spirit (1 + caster's Spirit). \\
\textbf{Special:} \textit{Incorporeal Movement} — passes through small gaps and obstacles. \textit{Sunlight Sensitivity} — while in direct sunlight, all actions are Desperate.
\end{tabular}

\subsubsection{Greater Spirit (e.g., Flame Elemental, Stone Guardian)}
\vspace{0.2em}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Cap:} 3 & \textbf{Harm:} 3 (area or single target) \\
\textbf{Dice:} 7d in primary domain (e.g., Body + Melee for Guardian, Spirit + Arcana for Elemental) \\
\textbf{Armor:} Stone Guardian — Heavy (Harm 3 $\rightarrow$ Fatigue 2); Flame Elemental — Light (Harm 1 $\rightarrow$ Fatigue 1) + immunity to fire. \\
\textbf{Leash:} Cap + Spirit (3 + caster's Spirit). \\
\textbf{Special:} \textit{Elemental Aura} — creatures in Close range suffer 1 Fatigue per round. \textit{Immutable} — cannot be poisoned or diseased.
\end{tabular}

\subsubsection{Wraith (Tier II–III)}
\vspace{0.2em}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Cap:} 2–3 & \textbf{Harm:} 2 (draining touch) + 1 Fatigue \\
\textbf{Dice:} Stealth 6d (Wits 3 + Stealth 3), Spirit + Resolve 6d (Spirit 3 + Resolve 3) \\
\textbf{Armor:} None (incorporeal — half physical Harm). \\
\textbf{Special:} \textit{Life Drain} — on a successful touch attack, the target marks 1 Fatigue and the wraith clears 1 Fatigue. \textit{Unquiet} — cannot cross a line of salt or a [WARD] without testing (DV = Cap).
\end{tabular}

\subsection{Animal NPCs}

\subsubsection{ Wolf (Tier I–II)}
\vspace{0.2em}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Cap:} 1–2 (pack) & \textbf{Harm:} 2 (bite) \\
\textbf{Dice:} Melee 5d (Body 3 + Brawl 2), Athletics 5d (Body 3 + Athletics 2) \\
\textbf{Armor:} None (hide). \\
\textbf{Special:} \textit{Pack Tactics} — when two or more wolves attack the same target, each gains +1 die. \textit{Blood Scent} — +2 dice to track wounded prey.
\end{tabular}

\subsubsection{Dire Bear (Tier III)}
\vspace{0.2em}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Cap:} 4 & \textbf{Harm:} 4 (claw or bite) \\
\textbf{Dice:} Melee 8d (Body 4 + Brawl 4), Endurance 8d (Body 4 + Endurance 4) \\
\textbf{Armor:} Medium (thick hide) — Harm 2 $\rightarrow$ Fatigue 1. \\
\textbf{Special:} \textit{Terrifying Roar} — all creatures in Near range must test Resolve (DV 4) or become Shaken. \textit{Maul} — on a critical hit, the target is knocked prone and must spend an action to stand.
\end{tabular}

\subsection{Building Custom NPCs}

Use these guidelines to create your own NPCs quickly.

\subsubsection{Step 1: Choose Cap}
Cap determines overall threat level and dice pool size.
\begin{itemize}
  \item Cap 1–2: Minions, guards, minor spirits. Dice pools 3–5.
  \item Cap 3–4: Elites, knights, greater spirits. Dice pools 6–8.
  \item Cap 5: Bosses, legendary creatures. Dice pools 9–10.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Step 2: Set Harm Output}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Light weapon or minor attack: Harm 1–2.
  \item Standard weapon or magical strike: Harm 2–3.
  \item Heavy weapon or powerful magic: Harm 3–4.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Step 3: Assign Armor}
\begin{itemize}
  \item None: No conversion.
  \item Light: Harm 1 $\rightarrow$ Fatigue 1.
  \item Medium: Harm 2 $\rightarrow$ Fatigue 1.
  \item Heavy: Harm 3 $\rightarrow$ Fatigue 2.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Step 4: Add 1–2 Special Abilities}
Examples: \textit{Charge}, \textit{Poison}, \textit{Fear Aura}, \textit{Regeneration}, \textit{Spellcasting (Low Rite)}.

\subsubsection{Step 5: Set Leash (for spirits)}
Leash = Cap + Spirit (use Spirit 2–4 for typical spirits).
