This appendix provides ready-to-use examples for each magical path. DVs and costs are guidelines; GMs may adjust based on fiction.

\subsection{Runekeeper Rites (by Patron)}

Runekeepers learn Rites from their Codex. Each Rite is invoked as 1 action and marks Obligation (+1 standard, +2 for Push).

\midrule

\subsection{Invoker Rituals (by Symbol)}

Invokers perform rituals using a Patron's Symbol. Rituals take DV+1 rounds and always mark +1 Obligation. Crack the Seal (instant) marks +2 Obligation (+3 for High) and renders the Symbol Compromised.

\begin{tabular}{@{}l>{\raggedright\arraybackslash}p{3cm}>{\raggedright\arraybackslash}p{7cm}@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Ritual} & \textbf{Patron} & \textbf{Effect} \\
\midrule
Oath-Ward (Low) & Any with Law/Ward domain & Chalk circle wards a small area (10ft radius). Violators test Spirit (DV 4) or cannot enter. \\
Wayfinder's Thread (Low) & The Traveler & Knot a red cord thrice; create a tether to a safe path for one leg. \\
Salt-Cut (Low) & The Sealed Gate & Sever a simple ongoing magical effect (rope, charm, weak ward). Consumes salt. \\
River's Memory (Standard) & Ra\'eyn & Scry a recent event tied to a personal token. Blurry but accurate. Costs 1 Fatigue. \\
Bargain-Bead (Standard) & Any Patron & Invite a minor spirit to parley. Requires two carved beads; one consumed. \\
Purge (Standard) & Oath of Flame \& Light & Cleanse one level of taint, curse, or corruption from a creature or object. Caster marks 1 Fatigue. \\
Summoner's Gate (High) & Any with Summoning domain & Call a known entity safely. Requires circle and true name. Occupies a concurrency slot. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}

\subsection{Cantor Songs}

Cantors perform Low Rites as Songs. Singing takes 1 action; the effect resolves at the start of the caster's next turn. Push (mark Fatigue + advance Corruption) resolves immediately.

\begin{tabular}{@{}l>{\raggedright\arraybackslash}p{3cm}>{\raggedright\arraybackslash}p{7cm}@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Song} & \textbf{Patron / Theme} & \textbf{Effect} \\
\midrule
Hearth's Lullaby & Mercy, Rest & All allies within Near recover 1 Fatigue. \\
Storm's Call & Weather, Sea & For one leg, treat weather hazards as one step milder (does not stack). \\
Raven's Omen & Shadow, Secrets & One ally gains +1 die to their next Stealth or Subterfuge roll. \\
Knot of Silence & Binding, Ward & Muffle all sound within Close range for one exchange. \\
Thread of Memory & Knowledge, Truth & Ask the GM one yes/no question about a past event in the current scene. \\
Bitter Dirge & Death, Endings & One enemy within Near suffers –1 die to their next action (fear). \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}

Any character with the time, components, and a skill roll (usually Spirit + Lore) may attempt these rituals.

Oath-Ward & 5 min, chalk circle & Ward small area; violators test Spirit (DV 4) to enter. \\
Ember-Glass & 1 min, smoked glass & Sense heat sources through cover for one scene. \\

Dream-Way Marker & 10 min, inked sleep mask, water cadence & Mark safe entrance to the Ways Between; short transit possible. All wake Shaken. \\
Purge (Tainted Object) & 10 min, bitter herbs, clean blade & Cleanse one level of taint from a small object. Caster takes minor backlash. \\
\bottomrule
