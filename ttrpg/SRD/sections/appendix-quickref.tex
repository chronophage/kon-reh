\subsection{Summoning Leash}

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Capacity:} Cap + Spirit segments.
  \item \textbf{Ticks:}
  \begin{itemize}
    \item Spirit takes Harm: 1 tick per Harm level.
    \item Command against nature: 1 tick.
    \item Split focus: 1 tick.
    \item Crosses a ward without dispelling: ticks = ward's DV.
  \end{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Leash fills:} Spirit acts once to its nature, then departs.
  \item \textbf{Boon Finesse:} Spend 1 Boon to clear 1 tick (once per round).
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Action Economy (Quick)}

\begin{itemize}
  \item Each turn: \textbf{1 Action + 1 Move}.
  \item \textbf{Move:} shift one range band (Close $\leftrightarrow$ Near $\leftrightarrow$ Far).
  \item \textbf{Dash (Action):} move two bands; next defense Desperate.
  \item \textbf{Disengage (Action):} then Move does not provoke opportunity attacks; cannot Attack this turn.
  \item \textbf{Charge/Skirmish (Full Turn):} move two bands and attack; costs 1 Fatigue; next defense Desperate; requires open ground.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Opportunity Attacks}

Moving out of an enemy's Close range without Disengage provokes one free melee attack (Controlled Position). The following actions also provoke if performed while engaged in Close with an enemy:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Making a ranged attack.
  \item Casting a spell without \texttt{[TOUCH]} or \texttt{[WARD]}.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Story Beat (SB) Spend Menu}

\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{SB Cost} & \textbf{Effect} \\
\midrule
1 & Minor pressure: noise, trace, tick a 4–6 segment timer +1. \\
2 & Moderate setback: alarm raised, lose Position, lesser foe appears. \\
3 & Serious trouble: reinforcements, key gear breaks, advance a campaign timer. \\
4+ & Major turn: trap springs, authority arrives, environment shifts dramatically. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}

\subsection{Boons at a Glance}

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Earn:} Partial = 1 Boon; Miss = 2 Boons.
  \item \textbf{Hold:} max 5; trim to 2 at scene end.
  \item \textbf{Spend 1 Boon:} re-roll a die, activate an Asset, improve Position by one step.
  \item \textbf{Spend 2 Boons:} convert to 1 XP (downtime, max 2 XP per session).
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Default Values (GM Cues)}

\begin{itemize}
  \item Default DV = 3.
  \item Default Position = Controlled.
  \item Default Effect = Standard.
  \item Default Range = Near.
\end{itemize}
