\section{Example Characters}

These Tier I (32 XP) characters illustrate core builds for different playstyles. Each includes attributes, skills, talents, equipment, a bond, and a complication.

\subsection{Ellis — Aeler Initiate (Warrior/Runekeeper)}

\textit{A young vent−prior's apprentice who left the underways to prove that stone−law can walk the surface world without breaking.}

⫕section*{Attributes}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
Body 3 & Wits 2 \\
Spirit 3 & Presence 2 \\
\end{tabular}

⫕section*{Skills}
Melee 2, Athletics 1, Endurance 1, Lore 1, Craft 1, Survival 1

⫕section*{Talents (12 XP total)}
\begin{itemize}
  ℹtem \textbf{Familiar (Thiasos) (2 XP)} — A small iron bell that hums when stone is near.
  ℹtem \textbf{Codex (4 XP)} — Sealed with a keystone rune; contains the Rites of the Sealed Gate.
  ℹtem \textbf{Weapon Master (5 XP)} — +2 dice with warhammer; once/scene +1 Effect.
  ℹtem \textbf{Effortless Charge (4 XP)} — Ignore Fatigue cost of Charge once/scene.
\end{itemize}

⫕section*{Equipment}
\begin{itemize}
  ℹtem Warhammer (Medium melee, Harm 2, +1d at Close, +2d at Near).
  ℹtem Iron Half−Plate (Medium armor, 1 Harm $→$ 1 Fatigue, 2 Harm $→$ 1 Fatigue).
  ℹtem Codex (iron−bound slate).
  ℹtem Breath−counter bell.
\end{itemize}

⫕section*{Bond}
``I owe Vent−Prior Orla a debt of stone and silence. She taught me that the mountain listens even when we do not speak.''

⫕section*{Complication}
\textbf{Stone−Press Susceptible:} In deep underground spaces, any failed roll ticks a personal Hallucination Timer [4].

⫕section*{Rites Known}
\begin{itemize}
  ℹtem \textbf{Sealed Threshold} (Low) — Ward a doorway; unauthorized crossing requires Resolve test.
  ℹtem \textbf{Oath−Ward} (Low) — Chalk circle wards a small area vs intrusion.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Lyra — Vilikari Rover (Rogue/Scout)}

\textit{A border−runner who learned to read two ledgers before she could write her name, now carries secrets instead of cargo.}

⫕section*{Attributes}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
Body 2 & Wits 4 \\
Spirit 2 & Presence 3 \\
\end{tabular}

⫕section*{Skills}
Stealth 2, Subterfuge 2, Sway 2, Investigation 1, Tinker 1, Insight 1

⫕section*{Talents (12 XP total)}
\begin{itemize}
  ℹtem \textbf{Keen Senses (2 XP)} — +1 die on perception checks to spot danger or hidden details.
  ℹtem \textbf{Trackless Step (2 XP)} — Once/scene, leave no trail for the remainder of the scene.
  ℹtem \textbf{Silk Palms (2 XP)} — When you bribe a minor official, gain temporary String \textit{Bought Silence}.
  ℹtem \textbf{Backstab (6 XP)} — When attacking from stealth, deal +1 Harm and ignore 1 point of Armor.
\end{itemize}

⫕section*{Equipment}
\begin{itemize}
  ℹtem Paired daggers (Light melee, Harm 1, +2d at Close, +1d at Near).
  ℹtem Padded leather (Light armor, 1 Harm $→$ 1 Fatigue, 2 Harm $→$ 1 Harm + 1 Fatigue).
  ℹtem Lockpicks (Tinker kit).
  ℹtem False manifest (forged papers).
\end{itemize}

⫕section*{Bond}
``Rurik the fence saved me from a customs trap. He expects me to return the favor when the ledger calls.''

⫕section*{Complication}
\textbf{Two−Ledger Debt:} You keep a second ledger of favors owed to Vilikari smugglers. The GM may once per session tick a \textit{Favor Due} timer [4] when you refuse a questionable job.

\subsection{Sera — Lethai−thora Exile (Caster)}

\textit{A former archivist who touched a forbidden scroll and now hears whispers from the space between written words.}

⫕section*{Attributes}
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
Body 2 & Wits 4 \\
Spirit 3 & Presence 2 \\
\end{tabular}

⫕section*{Skills}
Arcana 2, Lore 3, Insight 2, Investigation 1, Deception 1

⫕section*{Talents (10 XP total)}
\begin{itemize}
  ℹtem \textbf{Spellcraft (6 XP)} — Enables freeform casting (Weave + Cast).
  ℹtem \textbf{Long Memory (3 XP)} — +1 die to Lore and Insight rolls involving historical knowledge.
  ℹtem \textbf{Cold Reading (3 XP)} — +1 die to Sway and Insight when observing before acting.
\end{itemize}

⫕section*{Equipment}
\begin{itemize}
  ℹtem Quarterstaff (Light melee, Harm 1, +2d at Close, can Defend as action).
  ℹtem Scholar's robes (no armor).
  ℹtem Sealed scroll case (contains one forbidden fragment).
  ℹtem Brass inkpot (focus for [REVEAL] effects).
\end{itemize}

⫕section*{Bond}
``Keeper Lian hid me when the Censorate came. Her silence protects me; my voice will one day protect her secret.''

⫕section*{Complication}
\textbf{Whisper−Threshold:} Whenever you cast a spell that generates two or more SB, you hear a voice from the Ninth Scroll. The GM gains a String: \textit{The Archive Knows Your Name}.

⫕section*{Signature Spells}
\begin{itemize}
  ℹtem \textbf{Whisper−Light} ([FIRE][ILLUMINATE]) — DV 3, one action. Small, silent light.
  ℹtem \textbf{Memory's Veil} ([WATER][VEIL]) — DV 3, two actions. Obscure one small object or door; –1 die to Notice.
  ℹtem \textbf{Scroll's Rebuke} ([FATE][STRIKE]) — DV 3, two actions. Ranged Harm 1 (psychic), ignores mundane armor.
\end{itemize}
