\section{Welcome to Fate's Edge}

\noindent
Fate's Edge is a narrative-first tabletop RPG where every action has a cost, the fiction leads the rules, and tension is managed with \emph{Position}, \emph{Effect}, and \emph{Story Beats (SB)}. This quickstart gives you the complete table loop and ready-to-run tools.

% -----------------------------------------------------
\section{Core Resolution}

\subsection{The Art of Consequence}
All significant actions follow three steps:
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Approach}: Player states intent and method in the fiction.
  \item \key{Execution}: Roll a pool = \emph{Attribute + Skill} (d10s).\\
        Each 6–10 = 1 success. Each die showing 1 = 1 \emph{SB} to the GM.
  \item \key{Outcome}: Compare successes vs \emph{DV} (Difficulty Value).\\
        GM resolves using \emph{Position \& Effect} and spends SB for complications.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Attributes (1–5)}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Body}: Strength, exertion, taking hits
  \item \key{Wits}: Perception, speed of thought, timing
  \item \key{Spirit}: Will, intuition, focus
  \item \key{Presence}: Poise, command, social force
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Skills (0–5)}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Melee}, \key{Ranged}, \key{Athletics}
  \item \key{Sway}, \key{Subterfuge}, \key{Insight}
  \item \key{Stealth}, \key{Survival}, \key{Command}
  \item \key{Arcana}, \key{Lore}, \key{Craft} (Tinker)
\end{itemize}

\begin{fatebox}[Subterfuge, Stealth, and Craft (Tinker)]
\key{Subterfuge} = disguise, forgery, cons, planted evidence, social camouflage.\\
\key{Stealth} = not being seen or heard while moving or waiting.\\
\key{Craft (Tinker)} = devices, locks, traps, seals, wards, and tools.\\[3pt]
\emph{Guidance:} Paper and posture $\rightarrow$ \key{Subterfuge}. Avoiding notice $\rightarrow$ \key{Stealth}. Pins and gears $\rightarrow$ \key{Craft (Tinker)}.
\end{fatebox}

\subsection{Difficulty Values (DV)}
\begin{center}
\small
\begin{tabular}{cl}
\toprule
\textbf{DV} & \textbf{Situation} \\
\midrule
2 & Routine: clear intent, modest stakes \\
3 & Pressured (default): time pressure or mild resistance \\
4 & Hard: hostile conditions or active opposition \\
5+ & Extreme: multiple constraints, high precision \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\subsection{Position \& Effect}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Position}: \emph{Dominant} (safe), \emph{Controlled} (default), \emph{Desperate} (high stakes).
  \item \key{Effect}: \emph{Limited} (small), \emph{Standard} (expected), \emph{Great} (big).
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Shifting Position}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Improve}: advantage, leverage, tools, solid prep, or spend a Boon ($+1$ step).
  \item \key{Worsen}: alarms, pressure, exposure, or GM SB spend ($-1$ step).
\end{itemize}

\begin{examplebox}[Evolving Position]
\key{Start}: Dominant (quiet corridor).\\
\key{Noisy pick (1 SB)} $\rightarrow$ Controlled (patrol turns).\\
\key{Reinforcements (2 SB)} $\rightarrow$ Desperate (crossfire).
\end{examplebox}

\subsection{Outcome Matrix}
\begin{center}
\small
\begin{tabular}{ll}
\toprule
\textbf{Result} & \textbf{What Happens} \\
\midrule
Successes $\ge$ DV, SB=0 & Clean Success: intent achieved \\
Successes $\ge$ DV, SB$\ge$1 & Success \& Cost: intent + complications \\
0 $<$ Successes $<$ DV & Partial: progress with complication (gain 1 Boon) \\
Successes $=$ 0 & Miss: no progress (gain 2 Boons) \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\begin{fatebox}[Story Beats (SB): A Simple Spend Menu]
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{1 SB} — Minor pressure: noise, trace, drop a tool, tick a 4/6 clock +1.
  \item \key{2 SB} — Moderate setback: lose Position, raise alarm, harsher terms.
  \item \key{3 SB} — Serious trouble: reinforcements, break an asset, split the party.
  \item \key{4+ SB} — Major turn: authority arrives, environment shifts, Patron interferes.
\end{itemize}
\end{fatebox}

% -----------------------------------------------------
\section{Description Ladder}

How you act matters:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Basic}: roll as-is (all 1s generate SB).
  \item \key{Detailed}: re-roll one die showing 1 (both the original 1 and any new 1s generate SB).
  \item \key{Intricate}: re-roll all 1s; add a flourish on success (all 1s still count for SB).
\end{itemize}

% -----------------------------------------------------
\section{Boons: Reward for Risk}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{On Miss (0 successes)}: gain \textbf{2 Boons}.
  \item \key{On Partial}: gain \textbf{1 Boon}.
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Spending Boons}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Re-roll one die in any pool
  \item Activate an on-screen Asset
  \item Improve Position by 1 step
  \item Convert 2 Boons $\rightarrow$ 1 XP (max 2 XP/session)
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Limits}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Hold up to 5 Boons (trim to 2 at scene end)
  \item Max 2 Boons from failure per scene
\end{itemize}

% -----------------------------------------------------
\section{Action Economy}

Each turn:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{1 Action}: attack, cast, use a skill, or similar task.
  \item \key{1 Move}: shift one Range Band (Close$\leftrightarrow$Near or Near$\leftrightarrow$Far). \emph{Dash} (spend the Action) = move two bands.
\end{itemize}
Talents may grant bonus actions or reactions. Channeled magic may require multiple actions. Fatigue or harm can restrict options.

% -----------------------------------------------------
\section{Combat System}

Same core loop, now framed for danger.

\subsection{Procedure}
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Declare action and approach
  \item GM sets \emph{Position} and \emph{Effect}
  \item Roll Attribute + Skill dice
  \item Count successes vs DV (1s generate SB)
  \item GM resolves and spends SB
\end{enumerate}

\begin{fatebox}[Combat Position]
\emph{Dominant}: advantage, cover, tempo \quad
\emph{Controlled}: default exchange \quad
\emph{Desperate}: flanked, exposed, or collapsing ground
\end{fatebox}

\subsection{Turn Order}
No fixed initiative. Spotlight follows fiction:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Ambushers act first; targets respond after the opening beat.
  \item Players may propose acting when it makes sense.
  \item GM shifts spotlight to maintain tension and clarity.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Fatigue}\label{subsec:fatigue}
\key{Track}: Fatigue = \emph{Body}. Mark for exertion, strain, backlash.\\
\key{Effect}: Each Fatigue step worsens Position by one level (Dominant $\to$ Controlled $\to$ Desperate). If already \emph{Desperate}, take \emph{--1 die} per additional Fatigue.\\
\key{Overflow}: When full, increase \emph{Harm} by 1 step and clear all Fatigue.\\
\key{Recovery}: Short rest clears 1–2; full night clears all.

\subsection{Harm}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Level 1}: Minor (–1 die to related actions)
  \item \key{Level 2}: Serious (–1 die to most actions)
  \item \key{Level 3}: Critical (incapacitated/dying)
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Tactical Clocks}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Mob Overwhelm [6]}
  \item \key{Fatigue Spiral [4]}
  \item \key{Morale Collapse [6]}
  \item \key{Environmental Collapse [8]}
\end{itemize}

\section{Magic System}

\subsection{Three Paths}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Caster (Freeform)}: \emph{Weave} elements + \emph{Channel} power. (Requires \emph{Caster's Gift}.)
  \item \key{Runekeeper (Rites)}: Patron-bound rites with \emph{Obligation}. (Requires \emph{Codex}; \emph{Thaisos} shapes practice.)
  \item \key{Invoker (Symbols)}: Patron \emph{Seals} and rituals with time or risk.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Casting At a Glance}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Caster}: \emph{Channel + Weave} = 2 actions (or \emph{Push} to compress with cost).
  \item \key{Runekeeper}: 1 action to \emph{Invoke}; may \emph{Push} for +1 Obligation.
  \item \key{Invoker}: Rituals take turns; \emph{Crack the Seal} for instant cast at \emph{Desperate}.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Interrupt \& Push}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Interrupted}: If harmed/silenced before resolution, spell/rite fails.
  \item \key{Push}: Resolve now, mark +1 Fatigue or +1 Obligation/Corruption.
\end{itemize}

\begin{examplebox}[On-the-Fly Adjudication]
\emph{Barrier} for a hallway? Personal scale $\Rightarrow$ DV 2; scene-wide $\Rightarrow$ DV 3; sweeping area $\Rightarrow$ DV 4+. Casting under fire $\Rightarrow$ Controlled or Desperate.
\end{examplebox}

\section{Character Creation}

\subsection{Starting Build}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{30 XP} to spend
  \item Attributes 1–3; Skills 0–2
  \item Bonds (+2 XP) and Complications (+4 XP) optional
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Key Talents}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Caster's Gift (2 XP)}: access to freeform magic
  \item \key{Familiar (2 XP)}: Patron features access
  \item \key{Codex (4 XP)}: Runekeeper rites \& Obligation (\emph{Thaisos} defines style)
  \item \key{Patron's Symbol (4 XP)}: Invoker seals/rituals
\end{itemize}

% -----------------------------------------------------
\section{Range Bands \& Movement}

\subsection{Range Bands}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Close}: arm's reach
  \item \key{Near}: same room/space
  \item \key{Far}: line-of-sight distance
  \item \key{Absent}: off-screen (requires travel)
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Movement}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item 1 Move = shift one band (Close$\leftrightarrow$Near or Near$\leftrightarrow$Far)
  \item \emph{Dash} (spend Action) = shift two bands
  \item Melee Flag: mark engaged foes in \emph{Near} range
\end{itemize}

% -----------------------------------------------------
\section{Travel Framework}

\subsection{Procedure}
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Break journey into legs with \key{Travel Clock [4]}
  \item Assign roles: \key{Guide}, \key{Scout}, \key{Quartermaster}, \key{Watch}
  \item Advance clock via actions/encounters
  \item Resolve complications when the clock fills
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Travel Roles}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Guide}: on success, DV –1 for movement challenges
  \item \key{Scout}: on success, ignore the next 1 SB spend
  \item \key{Quartermaster}: on success, reduce Supplies Clock by 1
  \item \key{Watch}: on success, start next scene at \emph{Dominant}
\end{itemize}

% -----------------------------------------------------
\section{Deck of Consequences (Optional)}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Hearts} — Social/emotional fallout
  \item \key{Spades} — Physical harm/danger
  \item \key{Clubs} — Resource/fatigue pressure
  \item \key{Diamonds} — Arcane/supernatural turns
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Triggers (Draw 1; max 3/scene)}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item A \emph{Desperate} roll succeeds with cost
  \item GM spends \emph{2+ SB at once}
  \item A major clock fills (scene pivot)
\end{itemize}

\section{Advancement}

\subsection{XP Awards}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Standard: 6–10 XP / session
  \item Major objectives: +2–4 XP
  \item Discovery: +1–2 XP
  \item Hard choices: +1–2 XP
  \item Milestones: +8–12 XP / arc
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Spending XP}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Attributes: new rating $\times$ 3 XP
  \item Skills: new level $\times$ 2 XP
  \item Talents: as listed (2–6+ XP)
  \item Followers: Cap$^{2}$ XP
\end{itemize}

\section{GM Quick Reference}

\subsection{Adjudication Loop}
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Set Position and DV
  \item Roll Attribute + Skill (6+ = success; 1 = SB)
  \item Check Outcome Matrix
  \item Spend SB to escalate
  \item Advance clocks, move the fiction
\end{enumerate}

\begin{fatebox}[No-Thinking SB Spend]
\key{1 SB}: minor pressure (\emph{worsen Position} \emph{or} tick 4/6 +1).\\
\key{2 SB}: remove cover, split the party, tick environment +2.\\
\key{3 SB}: reinforcements, break an asset, force a hard choice.\\
\key{4+ SB}: scene transformation, authority arrival, collapse/eruption.
\end{fatebox}

\subsection{Five-Second Position \& Effect}
Ask two questions: \emph{What is the risk?} (Dominant/Controlled/Desperate). \emph{What is the impact?} (Limited/Standard/Great). Decide, roll.

\subsection{Lazy DV Table}
If in doubt: \key{DV = 3}. Raise/lower by 1 based on pressure or precision.

\subsection{Clocks: The Three-Clock Rule}
Keep at most three active:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Scene} (e.g., Guards Alerted [4])
  \item \key{Journey} (e.g., Mist Encroaches [6])
  \item \key{Campaign} (e.g., Baron's Suspicion [8])
\end{itemize}

\section{Worked Examples}

\begin{examplebox}[Lockpick Under Pressure]
\key{Situation}: Picking a warded lock while guards patrol nearby.\\
\key{Roll}: Wits 2 + Craft (Tinker) 2 = 4d10 $\rightarrow$ 8, 6, 3, 1.\\
\key{Result}: 2 successes (DV 2), 1 SB.\\
\key{Outcome}: Lock opens; GM spends 1 SB: the last pin clicks too loud—boots turn your way. Position worsens to \emph{Controlled}.
\end{examplebox}

\begin{examplebox}[False Orders, Real Panic]
\key{Situation}: Planting forged orders to redirect a patrol.\\
\key{Roll}: Wits 3 + Subterfuge 2 = 5d10.\\
\key{On Success}: Patrol peels off; Effect improves if you also present matching seals (\emph{Craft} assist).\\
\key{On Partial}: Rumor [2] starts against you; you gain 1 Boon.\\
\key{On Miss}: The captain cross-checks—GM spends SB to tighten security.
\end{examplebox}

\begin{examplebox}[From Dominant to Desperate]
\key{Setup}: Thane fires from cover (Dominant).\\
\key{Roll}: 6d10 $\rightarrow$ 10, 9, 7, 6, 3, 1 (SB=1). Clean hit (Great).\\
\key{SB Spend}: 1 SB—return fire chips the pillar, reducing cover. Next ally acts at \emph{Controlled}. Two more SB later, a mob arrives; the scene slips to \emph{Desperate}.
\end{examplebox}

\section{Starter Scenario: The Lantern at Dusk}

\subsection*{Premise}
Escort a relic lantern across the haunted Duskway. When lit, forgotten souls stir. Patrons may contest the route.

\subsection*{Setup}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Party at \key{Tier I} (or use pregens).
  \item Relic Lantern: on loss, trigger Patron consequence.
  \item Prepare a \key{Travel Clock [4]} for the Duskway.
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Encounter Seeds}
\paragraph{1. Shattered Bridge} DV 3 (Athletics/Survival/Craft). \emph{SB}: falling stones, time pressure. On failure: clock +1.
\paragraph{2. The Toll of Cinders} Parley (Presence+Sway) or trick (\emph{Wits+Subterfuge}). \emph{SB}: ambush, harsher terms.
\paragraph{3. Lantern Awakens} When Travel Clock fills, ghostlight swells: \emph{Restless Dead Mob [6]}. \emph{SB}: lantern leaks; terrain turns treacherous.

\subsection*{Scene Clocks}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Travel [4]} — legs of the road
  \item \key{Lantern Integrity [6]} — damage/corruption
  \item \key{Bandit Morale [4]} — collapses on fill
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Outcomes}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Success}: delivered; +6 XP, Patron favor, 1 contact
  \item \key{Mixed}: damaged; XP + ongoing Patron Obligation
  \item \key{Failure}: lost; +2 XP; introduce a Patron foe next session
\end{itemize}

\section{Session One Agenda (60–120 minutes)}

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*,label=\arabic*.]
  \item Cold open: concrete stakes (5–10m).
  \item Teach core loop with a short obstacle (10–15m).
  \item Run one travel leg or social scene (15–25m).
  \item Spotlight combat with clocks \& SB (25–40m).
  \item Debrief: XP, Boons, next hook (5–10m).
\end{enumerate}

\section{Designer’s Notes}

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \key{Fail Forward}: failure is story fuel, not a stall.
  \item \key{Fiction First}: description sets DV, Position, and Effect.
  \item \key{SB Economy}: spend often; let tension breathe.
  \item \key{Player Agency}: choices alter the map and the ledger.
\end{enumerate}
