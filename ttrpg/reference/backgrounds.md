## Background Creation Guidelines

### Structure Elements
1. **Required Asset:** Something concrete the character possesses or controls
2. **Signature Talent:** Unique ability that defines the background
3. **Perks:** Session-level benefits that reinforce theme
4. **Scene Keys:** Locations where the background provides advantage
5. **Hooks:** Narrative seeds that can drive story complications or opportunities

### Balance Principles
- **Fiction-First:** Backgrounds should enhance storytelling over raw mechanical power
- **Thematic Consistency:** All elements should reinforce the core concept
- **Limited Scope:** Perks should be situational, not universally applicable
- **Narrative Integration:** Hooks should create story opportunities rather than just advantages

### Integration Tips
- **Combine with Skills:** Backgrounds work best when they synergize with relevant skills
- **Support Multiple Play Styles:** Good backgrounds offer different approaches to challenges
- **Create Social Connections:** Backgrounds should provide NPC relationships and faction ties
- **Scale Appropriately:** Prestige backgrounds should feel truly exceptional and campaign-defining

These backgrounds provide players with rich character options that integrate deeply with Fate's Edge's setting while offering meaningful mechanical and narrative benefits.
