\section{Player−Facing Guidelines}
łabel{app:player}
\textbf{For All:} Position is everything. Earn Dominant by securing Diamonds first; roll later.

\medskip
\textbf{For Casters:} Backlash manifests as lost senses or skewed geometry; treat it as fiction, not a simple penalty.

\medskip
\textbf{For Runekeepers:} Your seals can anchor safety, but the entity hunts boundaries; expect targeted pressure.

\medskip
\textbf{For Invokers:} Parley with abstract aspects is possible; be ready to give up a symbol or habit to make it stick.

\medskip
\textbf{For Cantors:} Nonverbal music is still music. Conduct in silence; let hands be the choir.

\medskip
\textbf{For Summoners:} Spirits will ``remember'' wrong; let them test hazards, but mind their literalism.
