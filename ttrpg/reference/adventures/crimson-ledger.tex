\section*{Premise & Tone}
In the marble forums of Ecktoria, where the Utaran Empire's ghosts still echo through colonnaded halls, the city's greatest scandal isn't political−−−it's literary. The Crimson Codex, a legendary book of forbidden knowledge said to contain every secret, every lie, and every truth that could destroy the city's elite, has been stolen from the Library of Keys.

But this isn't just any theft. The Codex was bound in oaths−−−literally. Each page was a magically enforced secret, kept by the weight of sworn vows. Now those oaths are breaking, and as they do, Ecktoria's power structure is crumbling. Senators confess to treason in their sleep, merchants reveal the locations of their hidden vaults, and the High Priest of the Everflame begins preaching heretical sermons about the "true" history of the Empire.

\section*{The Hook}
The PCs are hired by Livaea, the Crimson Courtier, to recover the Codex before the city tears itself apart. But Livaea has her own agenda−−−the Codex contains the location of the Red Branch, a splinter faction of the old Utaran nobility who vanished centuries ago but may hold the key to restoring the Empire.

The twist? The theft was orchestrated by Inaea, Angel of the Spider, working through a network of blackmail victims who finally found the courage to fight back. The breaking oaths aren't a bug−−−they're a feature. And the Codex is writing itself as it's read, with each secret revealed adding new pages of devastating truths.

\section*{Fronts & Timers}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{Secrets Unbound [6/8]}
This timer tracks the cascading revelation of secrets throughout Ecktoria.
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem \textbf{Advances when:} Major secret revealed, failed social manipulation (DV 4+), Patron Rite affecting social structures, choosing Inaea's path over Livaea's
    ℹtem \textbf{Segments:}
    \begin{enumerate}
        ℹtem Minor scandals emerge; gossip spreads faster
        ℹtem Moderate revelations; one significant NPC makes public confession
        ℹtem Major social disruption; alliance breaks or forms
        ℹtem City−wide chaos; multiple factions in conflict
        ℹtem Institutional collapse; key organizations cease functioning
        ℹtem Catastrophic breakdown; city government paralyzed
    \end{enumerate}
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{Codex Rewriting [4/6]}
This timer represents the Codex's active response to events.
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem \textbf{Advances when:} PCs read 2+ pages, 3+ NPCs make major revelations, Patron's agenda advances significantly, Secrets Unbound advances 2+ segments
    ℹtem \textbf{Segments:}
    \begin{enumerate}
        ℹtem New minor secret appears in margins
        ℹtem Codex gains ability to reveal one specific truth
        ℹtem Major new chapter appears with game−changing information
        ℹtem Codex becomes actively hostile to readers
    \end{enumerate}
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\section*{The Crimson Codex − Mechanics}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{Reading the Codex}
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem \textbf{Risk:} Wits + Arcana DV 4. On failure, generate 1 SB. On partial, choose one:
    \begin{itemize}
        ℹtem Reveal truth but mark 1 Fatigue
        ℹtem Gain insight but generate 1 SB (Diamonds)
        ℹtem Learn secret but mark 1 SB (Hearts)
    \end{itemize}
    ℹtem \textbf{Rewards:} Successful reading grants:
    \begin{itemize}
        ℹtem +1d to Investigation about related topics
        ℹtem One specific truth about target NPC/faction
        ℹtem Advantage on social rolls against target (once)
    \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{Codex Backlash Table}
When reading fails or generates SB, roll 1d6:
\begin{enumerate}
    ℹtem Your secret revealed to nearby NPCs
    ℹtem Target of your investigation becomes aware
    ℹtem Patron (Livaea or Inaea) gains leverage over you
    ℹtem New complication timer starts (2−4 segments)
    ℹtem Harm 1 (Mental stress from forbidden knowledge)
    ℹtem Gain permanent Condition: Truth−Sensitive (−1d to deception)
\end{enumerate}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\section*{Potential Twists}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{The Red Branch's True Agenda}
The exiled nobles aren't returning to restore the Empire−−−they want to reclaim their lost artifacts and disappear again, using the chaos as cover. The Codex might be their key to finding these items.

\textbf{Reveal:} When PCs investigate the Red Branch, they discover plans for a "quiet extraction" rather than a glorious return.
\end{mechanicsbox}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{The Chosen Voice}
The High Priest isn't mad; she's been chosen by the Codex itself to be its voice, preaching the "truth" it contains.

\textbf{Reveal:} During a religious ceremony, her eyes turn crimson when reading from any text, and she speaks prophecies that later prove true.
\end{mechanicsbox}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{Personal Secrets}
A PC's own secret is contained within the Codex. Reading it or being near it causes them to involuntarily reveal it, or grants the Patron leverage over them.

\textbf{Implementation:} Each PC writes down one secret during character creation. When Codex Rewriting advances, GM may reveal one PC secret.
\end{mechanicsbox}

\section*{GM Guidance}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{Running the City}
Make Ecktoria feel alive by:
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem Describing changing atmospheres in districts
    ℹtem Having NPCs react differently based on district state
    ℹtem Using the Secrets Unbound timer to trigger district shifts
    ℹtem Letting player actions have visible consequences on city mood
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{Patron Balance}
Present both Patrons' offers as:
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem Equally tempting within their frameworks
    ℹtem Logically consistent with their natures
    ℹtem Carrying clear, immediate costs
    ℹtem Leading to different long−term consequences
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{Social Combat}
Treat social encounters like combat:
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem Establish clear stakes and consequences
    ℹtem Use Position to reflect social advantage
    ℹtem Let timers drive escalating tension
    ℹtem Reward creative solutions and roleplay
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\section*{Character Integration}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{For Runekeepers}
The abundance of oaths and secrets makes Oath−based Rites incredibly potent:
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem \textbf{Binding Vow:} Stabilize crumbling alliances
    ℹtem \textbf{Mark of Antlers:} Grant temporary authority in chaos
    ℹtem \textbf{Revelation:} Pierce illusions but risk unwanted truths
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{For Cantors}
The Cantor's Path can be used to "sing" truths or lies:
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem \textbf{Hymn of Truth:} Force honesty in social encounters (+1 Effect)
    ℹtem \textbf{Song of Lies:} Create convincing deceptions (+1d to Deception)
    ℹtem \textbf{Chorus of Unity:} Calm conflicts (+1 Position to peacemaking)
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{For Invokers}
Ritual magic excels at investigation and information gathering:
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem \textbf{Reveal Rites:} Expose hidden connections or concealed truths
    ℹtem \textbf{Ward Rites:} Protect against unwanted revelations
    ℹtem \textbf{Command Rites:} Compel truth from unwilling sources
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\section*{Session Flow Recommendations}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{Opening (30 minutes)}
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem Establish the chaos through NPC reactions
    ℹtem Present the initial hook from Livaea or contact
    ℹtem Start Secrets Unbound timer at 2 segments
    ℹtem Introduce 2−3 key NPCs with immediate problems
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{Midgame (2 hours)}
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem Focus on 2−3 major investigation threads
    ℹtem Advance timers through player choices
    ℹtem Introduce Patron offers and complications
    ℹtem Build toward Red Branch revelation
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{Climax (1 hour)}
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem Library confrontation with Codex
    ℹtem Patron final gambits
    ℹtem Player choice determines outcome
    ℹtem Resolve major NPC storylines
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}

\begin{mechanicsbox}{Resolution (30 minutes)}
\begin{itemize}
    ℹtem Show consequences of player choices
    ℹtem Award XP for engagement and roleplay
    ℹtem Discuss potential campaign expansion
\end{itemize}
\end{mechanicsbox}
