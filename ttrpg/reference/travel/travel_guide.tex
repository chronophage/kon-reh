\section{Core Travel Procedure}

For each leg of a journey, draw 3‚ąí‚ąí4 cards using the decks for your destination and controlling authority.

\begin{itemize}
‚ĄĻtem Spade from the destination deck: sets the scene (place).
‚ĄĻtem Heart from the destination deck: introduces the local actor or faction.
‚ĄĻtem Club from the Wilds (general hazards) or destination (if strongly policed): brings pressure.
‚ĄĻtem Diamond from the authority that gates the route: papers, escorts, rights, or exceptions.
\end{itemize}

\begin{itemize}
‚ĄĻtem 2‚ąí‚ąí5 $‚áí$ 4 segments

‚ĄĻtem J/Q/K $‚áí$ 8 segments
‚ĄĻtem A $‚áí$ 10 segments

On success, advance to the next leg; on failure, mark delay, debt, or diversion and resolve a consequence in the fiction.
