Fate’s Edge — Patron Compendium (34 Patrons)

A table-ready reference to every Patron currently in canon. Keep this beside your GM screen; each entry is tight but complete enough to adjudicate Gifts, omens, and frictions on the fly.

⸻

How to use this
	•	Domains — what the Patron governs.
	•	Gift — the feel of their Patron’s Gift (what the blessing looks like in play).
	•	Thematic Skill — the skill most often heightened by their Gift.
	•	Fear-Influence — how this Patron bends fear outcomes (Amplify / Reroll / Resonance / Transfer).
	•	Rivals / Friction — quick cues for who opposes them and where.
	•	Notes — table-facing color, common rites/omens, or a one-line callout for sea play.
