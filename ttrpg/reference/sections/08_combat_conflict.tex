\subsection{Running Social Timers (GM Guidance)}Ňāabel{subsec:social‚āúimers}
‚ąądex{Social conflict!Timers}‚ąądex{Persuasion}

\paragraph{Framing the Ask.}
State a concrete outcome ("grant passage tonight," "drop the bounty," "fund our expedition"). If it is strictly binary and low‚ÄĎstakes, use one roll. Otherwise, build a timer.

\paragraph{Set Two Timers.}

    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Persuasion} (4/6/8): your progress.
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Opposition} (4/6): their resistance (ego, risk, duty, rival's whisper).

\paragraph{Position $‚Üí$ DV.}

\begin{itemize}
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Dominant (DV 2):} private audience, proof in hand, shared values.
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Controlled (DV 3):} time pressure, partial access, mixed reception.
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Desperate (DV 4‚ąí‚ąí5+):} public scrutiny, scandal risk, hostile crowd.
\end{itemize}

\paragraph{Distinct Approaches (examples).}
\begin{itemize}
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Wits + Sway:} reframe incentives; offer face‚ÄĎsaving out.
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Wits + Lore:} cite precedent, produce documents or testimony.
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Body + Presence:} command presence, ritual authority, oath.
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Wits + Tinker:} demonstrate a device / proof‚ÄĎof‚ÄĎconcept on the spot.
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Bonds / Boons:} an ally vouches (assist) or gifts a Boon; NPC bonds can reduce DV by 1 if genuinely invoked.

\paragraph{Outcomes Palette.}

    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Strong Hit:} Tick Persuasion +2; optionally bank a \emph{Concession} (waive a future cost).
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Mixed:} Tick +1 and choose a cost: start a small \textsc{Rebuttal 1/4}, owe a minor favor, or the GM spends 1 SB to introduce a new stakeholder.
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Miss:} No progress. GM may (a) reduce Persuasion ‚ąí‚ąí1, (b) advance Opposition +1‚ąí‚ąí2, or (c) worsen Position one step.

\paragraph{When Opposition Fills First.}
The target hardens or reframes the negotiation: narrow the ask, accept a condition, or escalate proof (pay a Boon, reveal evidence, bring a witness) to keep going.

\paragraph{Scaling & Dials.}
\begin{itemize}
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Crowd Scenes:} Add \textsc{Hecklers} / \textsc{Applause} 1/4 that swing Position when they fill.
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Stake Weight:} For life‚ÄĎor‚ÄĎdeath asks, require a \emph{Concession} on success (named cost paid during wrap‚ÄĎup).
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Truth vs. Bluff:} Real proof improves Position; blatant lies risk a hidden \textsc{Caught Out} 1/4 that explodes later.
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Patron Colour:} Invoking a Patron symbol can shift Position if on‚ÄĎtheme‚ąí‚ąí‚ąíor add +1 DV if it antagonises the audience's loyalties.

\paragraph{End States.}

\section{Miniatures and Tactical Layer}Ňāabel{sec:miniatures}

\subsection{Core Concepts}

\subsection{Turn Structure}

\subsection{Zones of Control (ZoC)}Ňāabel{subsec:zoc}

    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Entry:} Entering an enemy's ZoC ends your movement (you are \textbf{Engaged}).
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Passage:} You cannot move \textit{through} an enemy's ZoC unless you \textbf{Push‚ÄĎthrough} (below) or \textbf{Disengage} (Action; DV 4).
\end{itemize}

‚ęēsection*{Push‚ÄĎThrough}

    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Test:} Body + Athletics vs. DV \(= 2 + \text{enemy Tier}\) (or Cap for followers / minions).
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Cost (choose one):}

            ‚ĄĻtem Drop Position by one step (min. Desperate) on next action this turn, \textbf{or}
            ‚ĄĻtem Suffer ‚ąí‚ąí1 die on next roll, \textbf{or}
            ‚ĄĻtem Grant enemy immediate opportunity attack at +1 die.
        \end{itemize}
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Fail:} Stop in their ZoC; movement ends.
    ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{Tier IV+:} Spend 1 Boon to ignore ZoC for one Move (treat as narrative space).
\end{itemize}
