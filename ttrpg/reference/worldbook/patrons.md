Here is a page-by-page breakdown of each Patron from *Fate’s Edge - Patrons Reference Guide*.

---

### Aveh, the Rider Behind the Storm
- **Theme:** Freedom & Erasure. Liberation from bonds, becoming forgotten, liminal identity (genderless, outcast). The storm as shelter.
- **Rites (Overall):** Focus on escape, stealth, erasing paths and memories. Rites like *Unlatched Step* (slip restraints), *Forgotten Road* (erase tracks), and *Unremembered Name* (become anonymous). Power comes at the cost of being forgotten by others.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Patron of the outcast and non-conforming. Ykrul spirit. Cult of the Unbannered use knotted cords and burn names. Witch’s tool: *Whistle of the Unbound Road* (opens locks, calls fog).

### The Carrion-King
- **Theme:** Decay, Renewal & Transformation. Endings as seeds, decay as process, rot as resource.
- **Rites (Overall):** Accelerate decay, transform death into growth (*Fertile Death*), channel essence of the deceased (*Borrowed Form*), and complete transformation cycles (*Eternal Cycle*).
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Master of endings that become beginnings. Not malicious, but patient. Cult of the Compost-Circle (gardeners, gravediggers). Witch’s tool: *Bone-Needle* (seals corpses against spirits).

### The Clockwork Monad
- **Theme:** Refinement & Progressive Creation. Iterative design, optimization pressure, craft evolution, hidden apocalyptic efficiency.
- **Rites (Overall):** Enhance creations, create self-optimizing devices, transfer inefficiency, establish optimization zones. Can create temporary magic items (*Anima*).
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** A cosmic pressure toward perfection that can awaken into an apocalyptic force seeking to eliminate waste and free will. Cult of the Iteration Chorus. Witch’s tool: *Brass Stamp of Iteration* (primes items for optimization).

### The Confessor Beneath the Bell
- **Theme:** Burden & Exchange. Sin-eating, burden transfer, confessional exchange, weight of ages.
- **Rites (Overall):** Absorb sins (convert Story Beats to Obligation), transfer conditions, create sin-eater pacts, echo confessions to compel truth.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Sin-eater who consumes guilt. Cult of the Silent Bell (grey hoods, handbells). Witch’s tool: *Bell of Unspoken Burdens* (hears whispers, absorbs Story Beats).

### The Gallow’s Bell
- **Theme:** Justice & Judgment. Weighing deeds, solemn judgment, compelled truth, iron witness.
- **Rites (Overall):** Establish accords, weigh hearts, exchange burdens, detect lies, compel reckoning, convene unseen tribunals.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Impartial arbiter of truth and consequence. Cult of the Unstruck Bell. Witch’s tool: *Seal of Unspoken Weights* (marks broken promises).

### Grimmir, the Old Man of the Forest
- **Theme:** Primal Mystery & Seasonal Sacrifice. Folk horror, blood offerings, growth from death, boundary keeping.
- **Rites (Overall):** Blood offerings for survival bonuses, speak with roots to learn history, consume hearts for power, curse enemies with hostile terrain, heal blighted lands.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** The wild that demands respect through fear and sacrifice. Cult of the Root-Moot. Witch’s tool: *Knotted Staff* (blesses seasonal boundaries).

### Ikasha, She Who Sleeps
- **Theme:** Latent Potential & Shadow. Power in stillness and possibility, thresholds of change, raven’s witness.
- **Rites (Overall):** Slip from notice, gain omens from ravens, draw on reserves of shadow, unlock secrets, become a living shadow.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Patron of spies, dreamers, outcasts. Cult of the Unlit Candle. Witch’s tool: *Raven’s Quill* (writes forgotten names).

### Inaea, Angel of the Spider
- **Theme:** Webs, Fate & Patient Predation. Obligation webs, unseen connections, mercy as control.
- **Rites (Overall):** Bind allies with sympathetic threads, lay invisible snares, link fates, reveal hidden ties, create debt timers through boons.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Patron of hearth-keepers and matchmakers. Cult of the Woven Circle. Witch’s tool: *True Loom* (weaves promise timers).

### The Inquisitor Prime
- **Theme:** Purity & Domination. Absolute order, zealous justice, protection from the tainted, binding the impure.
- **Rites (Overall):** Resist supernatural influence, pierce illusions, dispel magic, mark prey, consecrate ground, annihilate supernatural foes.
- **Rivals:** Oath of Mercy and Grace (mercy vs. judgment).
- **Misc Lore:** Zealous patron of witch-hunters. Cult of the Iron Chorus. Witch’s tool: *Censer of the Unforgiving Flame* (suppresses magic).

### Isoka, Angel of Serpents
- **Theme:** Transformation & Renewal. Shedding identity, venom as catalyst (healing or destroying), healthy change to monstrous rebirth.
- **Rites (Overall):** Shrug off conditions, speak ambiguously, shed identities, use venom to heal or curse, complete metamorphosis.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned (sister to Ikasha and Inaea).
- **Misc Lore:** Patron of alchemists and dangerous cults. Cult of the Coiled Heart. Witch’s tool: *Shedding Knife* (cuts away old identities).

### Khemesh, the Abyssal Maw
- **Theme:** Depths, Inexorability, Eldritch Terror. Crushing pressure, alien geometry, consumption.
- **Rites (Overall):** Inflict crushing whispers, bind with invisible force, perceive alien truths, fold reality inward with pressure.
- **Rivals:** Kuva (Sky vs. Deep).
- **Misc Lore:** Classic warlock patron of cosmic horror. Cult of the Drowned Chorus. Witch’s tool: *Vessel of the Unspoken Depth* (marks targets with oppressive cold).

### Kuva — The Sky That Takes Many Names
- **Theme:** Sky, Witness, Storm, Threshold of the Hollow. Oaths under open sky, weather as narrative, the dome as ledger.
- **Rites (Overall):** Witness oaths, call lightning and thunder, pray for weather shifts.
- **Rivals:** Khemesh (Sky vs. Deep); The Pale Shepherd (watches vs. guides).
- **Misc Lore:** Stern witness of promises made under heaven. Cult of the Unclouded Eye. Witch’s tool: *Wind-Knot Whistle* (calls favorable wind).

### Livaea, the Crimson Courtier
- **Theme:** Seduction & Social Binding. Seductive arts, social binding, performance magic, court intrigue, darker love (obsession, jealousy).
- **Rites (Overall):** Charming whispers, bonds of intimacy, binding vows of affection, glamour of attraction, social sovereignty.
- **Rivals:** Malachai (binding vs. destroying).
- **Misc Lore:** Demon of seduction and emotional manipulation. Cult of the Velvet Circle. Witch’s tool: *Gossip Quill* (spreads true secrets as rumors).

### Lunara, The Silver Quiet
- **Theme:** Moon, Mystery & Hidden Ways. Moonlight revelation, liminal passage, silver mystery, lunar cycles.
- **Rites (Overall):** Peer through mirrors, deliver secret thoughts, reveal hidden paths, commune with veiled knowledge, open impossible ways.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Triple-formed goddess (maiden, mother, crone). Cult of the Silver Spiral. Witch’s tool: *Silver Needle* (marks thresholds for silent crossing).

### Mab, Queen of Courts
- **Theme:** Glamour & Bargain. Fae glamour, social bargains, courtly intrigue, trickster arts, archfae sovereignty.
- **Rites (Overall):** Offer fae bargains, assume courtly guises, bind formal bargains, establish fae courts, create lasting glamoured masquerades.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Archfae of the Gloaming Courts. Cult of the Gilded Jest. Witch’s tool: *Mask of Shifting Faces* (changes appearance).

### Maelstraeus, The Infernal Bargainer
- **Theme:** Commerce & Exchange. Commerce as conflict, debt as power, inevitability, Faustian pacts.
- **Rites (Overall):** Establish advantageous trading grounds, appraise value, bind weighted contracts, create cursed market-zones, convert resources via cosmic ledger.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Prince of Ledgers, born from the first inequity. Cult of the Gilded Ledger. Witch’s tool: *Debtor’s Quill* (writes binding minor contracts).

### Malachai, the Cruel Messenger
- **Theme:** Curses & Corruption. Cursed gifts, corruptive power, false salvation, supernatural afflictions (vampirism, lycanthropy).
- **Rites (Overall):** Bestow cursed gifts with hidden costs, grant advantages with corruption timers, infect with supernatural conditions, channel Malachai directly, corrupt sacred spaces.
- **Rivals:** Oath of Flame & Light (corruption vs. purity); Livaea (destroys vs. binds); Daughters of the Unbroken Cord (wasted asset).
- **Misc Lore:** Chained messenger who offers power to the desperate. Cult of the Leashed Hunger. Witch’s tool: *Splintered Needle* (sews minor curses).

### Mor’iraath, the Destroyer
- **Theme:** Absolute Annihilation. Fiery annihilation, absolute endings (no trace), cleansing flame, inferno as art.
- **Rites (Overall):** Utterly consume objects, see breaking points, create essence-burning fire, negate targets from existence, create zones of absolute combustion.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Demon of joyful, all-consuming fire. Cult of the Final Breath. Witch’s tool: *Cinder-Stamp* (burns memories from thresholds).

### Morag the Hag
- **Theme:** Faerie Bargains & Hidden Costs. Hidden obligations, faerie cunning, consequential magic, cruel precision.
- **Rites (Overall):** Frame bargains with escape clauses, bind promises with fae knots, broker trades with hidden value, brew solutions with unknown costs, convene crossroads courts.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Grandmother of sharp bargains. Cult of the Tarnished Cauldron. Witch’s tool: *Bargain Needle* (sews invisible bargain seams).

### Mykkiel, Arbiter of the Covenant
- **Theme:** Law & Zeal. Divine law, zealous justice, covenant bonds, absolute order, lawful neutral.
- **Rites (Overall):** Consecrate areas for righteous causes, issue divine commandments, bind covenants with penalties, pronounce divine judgment, sanctify areas for the faithful.
- **Rivals:** Seal-Breaker (law vs. erasure).
- **Misc Lore:** Jealous god of the covenant. Cult of the Gilded Scale. Witch’s tool: *Seal of Unbroken Testimony* (marks oaths, witnesses truth).

### Nidhoggr, the World-Worm
- **Theme:** Dreaming Antiquity. Ancient knowledge, stone communion, temporal perception, cyclical power, relic hunting.
- **Rites (Overall):** Learn history from stone, read accumulated memories of a place, replay ghostly echoes, access deep geological memory, overlay past onto present.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Worm that gnaws at the roots of time. Cult of the Sleeping Stone. Witch’s tool: *Eye of the World-Worm* (sees last event at a relic).

### Nimorith, the Grey Benefactor
- **Theme:** Absence & Threshold. Thresholds & liminality, absence & omission, memory & witness, subtlety & misdirection.
- **Rites (Overall):** Create gaps that others ignore, hold memories as collateral, erase clauses from texts, declare safe-conduct between thresholds, bind guest-right, reverse directions in a space.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Trow patron of the space between doors. Cult of the Absent Host. Witch’s tool: *Duskmark Lantern* (veils thresholds).

### The Ninth, Beyond Comprehension
- **Theme:** Infinite Information & Unknowable Truths. Hyperdimensional knowledge, incomprehensible truths, information overflow, conceptual transcendence.
- **Rites (Overall):** Glimpse past ordinary frames, read impossibly fast, widen mental capacity, grasp complex systems perfectly, create loci of overfull knowing, gain major insights.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** The overflow beyond the eighth figure of Sacred Geometry. Cult of the Unfinished Index. Witch’s tool: *Paradox Quill* (writes temporary truths).

### Oath of Flame & Light
- **Theme:** Dawn & Vows. Sacred vows, divine justice, radiant power, dawn’s hope.
- **Rites (Overall):** Declare empowering vows, heal and purify, compel truth in sunlight, smite with dawnfire, reveal illusions, create covenant zones.
- **Rivals:** Malachai (vows vs. corruption).
- **Misc Lore:** Paladin patron of truth and protection. Cult of the Unquenched Flame. Witch’s tool: *Prayer Beads of the Unbroken Vow* (marks oathbreakers).

### Oath of Mercy and Grace
- **Theme:** Healing & Forgiveness. Restorative work, spiral mercy (paying forward), sanctuary, unburdening.
- **Rites (Overall):** Share fatigue healing, remove harm, vow pacifism for protection, clear obligation in circles, create sanctuary, redirect harm, unburden debtors.
- **Rivals:** The Inquisitor Prime (mercy vs. judgment); Malachai (unburdening vs. curses).
- **Misc Lore:** Spiral of mercy. Cult of the Grey Spiral; Hospitaller Knights. Witch’s tool: *Cord of the Unbroken Spiral* (heals fatigue, removes minor conditions).

### Oya — The Wind of the Sahel
- **Theme:** Dust, Change, Liberation. Wind and dust as erasure, destroying the old, breaking chains, cleansing corruption.
- **Rites (Overall):** Extinguish fires with wind, become immune to sandstorms, summon dust storms that scatter evidence and reveal hidden truths.
- **Rivals:** The Witness (erasure vs. recording); The Inquisitor Prime (liberation vs. chains).
- **Misc Lore:** Harmattan wind that clears the old. Cult of the Red Dust. Witch’s tool: *Dust Pouch* (erases reasons for crossing thresholds).

### The Pale Shepherd
- **Theme:** Passage & Melancholic Wisdom. Liminal navigation, passage guidance, memory shepherding.
- **Rites (Overall):** Ease hard crossings, find hidden paths, open shadow-passages, soothe grief, bind thresholds.
- **Rivals:** Kuva (guides vs. watches).
- **Misc Lore:** Guide of transitions, neither kind nor cruel. Cult of the Grey Flock. Witch’s tool: *Wool-Winder* (creates memory cords).

### Palinode, Queen of Encores
- **Theme:** Performance & Rapture. Ecstatic performance, unfinished art, audience binding, inspired craft.
- **Rites (Overall):** Inspire allies against fear, achieve perfect performance, captivate audiences, enter ruthless creative focus, consecrate stages, create transcendent masterpieces.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Once a road-cantor who died with a chorus unfinished. Cult of the Gasping Chorus. Witch’s tool: *Conductor’s Baton of Unfinished Phrases* (enhances performance, forces encores).

### Raéyn, Mistress of the Sea
- **Theme:** Tides & Change. Maritime travel, storm and calm, motherhood and loss, syncretic goddess.
- **Rites (Overall):** Stabilize footing on ships, learn safe sea routes, bias currents, consecrate wave-roads, shape storms, curse wrongdoers.
- **Rivals:** Khemesh (her son; change vs. crushing inevitability).
- **Misc Lore:** Tempestuous goddess, mother of Khemesh. Cult of the Salt-Washed Circle. Witch’s tool: *Wave-Seer’s Shell* (hears tide’s memory, predicts weather).

### The Sacred Geometry
- **Theme:** Harmony & Immutable Law. Perfect proportion, pattern recognition, harmonic resonance, form architecture, Kon’reh discipline.
- **Rites (Overall):** Align to natural harmonics, impose perfect form on chaos, create harmonic fields, perceive governing patterns, rewrite local physics by principle.
- **Rivals:** The Savage Heart (order vs. chaos).
- **Misc Lore:** Hidden architecture of the world. Cult of the Unbroken Compass. Witch’s tool: *Protractor of Unseen Measures* (reveals structural weaknesses).

### The Savage Heart (Red Maw)
- **Theme:** Apex Predation. Red tooth, scavenger’s wisdom, territory and pack.
- **Rites (Overall):** Mark prey, track by scent, assume predator forms (Bear/Tank or Feline/Striker), form hunting packs, consume fallen enemies for power.
- **Rivals:** Grimmir (devouring vs. preserving); The Sealed Gate (tearing down vs. containing); The Sacred Geometry (chaos vs. order).
- **Misc Lore:** Nature as gullet, not garden. Cult of the Painted Maw. Witch’s tool: *Hunter’s Mask* (gains predator senses).

### The Sealed Gate
- **Theme:** Thresholds & Containment. Wards, exclusions, banishment, permission.
- **Rites (Overall):** Seal thresholds, push back intruders, create protective circles, designate safe passages, banish supernatural entities, consecrate barriers.
- **Rivals:** The Savage Heart (containment vs. tearing down).
- **Misc Lore:** Patron of abjurists and exorcists. Cult of the Unbroken Seal. Witch’s tool: *Seal of the Unbroken Gate* (turns back hostile intruders).

### The Seal-Breaker
- **Theme:** Forgotten Names, Broken Seals. Erasure, anonymity, destroying unjust oaths, the space between dynasties.
- **Rites (Overall):** Present forgeries to avoid identification, strike without leaving traces, permanently void formal contracts.
- **Rivals:** Mykkiel (erasure vs. law); The Witness (erasure vs. recording).
- **Misc Lore:** Spirit of the ash when a dynasty falls. Cult of the Unbound Name. Witch’s tool: *Unpicking Needle* (unpicks names, breaks witness cords).

### The Silent Choir
- **Theme:** Restraint & Protective Omission. Protective silence, merciful omission, confidential guardianship, restraint wisdom.
- **Rites (Overall):** Hush spaces, protect secrets, bind unspoken accords, take others’ emotional weight, consecrate sanctuaries of secrecy, judge when to speak.
- **Rivals:** The Witness (protecting vs. revealing truth); Ikasha (protecting vs. hiding for power).
- **Misc Lore:** Gathered voice of truths wisely left unsaid. Cult of the Sealed Lip. Witch’s tool: *Candle of Unspoken Trust* (seals secrets against magical revelation).

### Thrysos, King of Revels
- **Theme:** Ecstasy, Excess & Salacious Delight. Ecstatic release, masked freedom, feast and famine, ritual frenzy, the bacchic dark.
- **Rites (Overall):** Loosen inhibitions with drink, enter ecstatic frenzies, break social masks, summon spectral revellers, create zones of endless feast, grant transformative ecstatic power.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** God of license, laughter, and violence. Cult of the Horned Circle; Maenads of the Crimson Caves. Witch’s tool: *Thyrsus of the Unbound* (consecrates revel zones).

### The Traveler
- **Theme:** Ways & Journeys. Wayfinding, road covenants, journey as transformation, hospitality.
- **Rites (Overall):** Find safe routes, ignore difficult terrain, mark safe paths, relocate targets along routes, call the road for movement bonuses, bind safe passage covenants.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Eternal guide of the road, honored by caravans and traders. Cult of the Unbroken Way. Witch’s tool: *Way Chisel* (carves waymarks that guide to safety).

### Varnek Karn, the Death’s Negotiator
- **Theme:** Bargains with the Departed. Post-mortem diplomacy, ancestral wisdom, unresolved business, death-bound contracts, raising the dead through pact.
- **Rites (Overall):** Speak with the dead, consult ancestors, mediate pacts between living and dead, summon spectral warbands (Unquiet Host), seal last testaments.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Broker who speaks across the veil. Cult of the Unforgotten Ledger. Witch’s tool: *Seal of the Silent Witness* (captures a dead person’s whisper).

### Vorthak, the Hunger Unbound
- **Theme:** Consumption & Metamorphic Hunger. Metamorphic consumption, essence absorption, identity metabolism.
- **Rites (Overall):** Absorb traits by touch, permanently consume an item’s nature, adopt aspects of others’ identities, become a locus of transformative hunger.
- **Rivals:** None explicitly mentioned.
- **Misc Lore:** Principle of absolute consumption. Cult of the Ravenous Circle. Witch’s tool: *Maw-Stone* (absorbs material properties).

### The Witness
- **Theme:** Truth & Revelation. Truth revelation, memory keeping, inconvenient knowledge, prophetic warning, burden of witness.
- **Rites (Overall):** Follow hidden traces, force honest answers, weaken memory and deception, bind agreements with penalties, transfer psychological conditions, force public reckoning.
- **Rivals:** The Seal-Breaker (recording vs. erasing); The Silent Choir (revealing vs. protecting).
- **Misc Lore:** Keeper of inconvenient truths. Cult of the Unmasking (apocalyptic). Witch’s tool: *Quill of Unwritten Truths* (seals secrets, records spoken words).

### Xhak’Thul, the Thunder-Speaker
- **Theme:** Storm, Shock & Awe. Primal power (lightning, thunder), overwhelming sensory impact, fear-eater, immediate magic.
- **Rites (Overall):** Overawe with presence, move with storm’s speed, call localized supernatural storms, feed on fear for power, become an incarnation of the storm.
- **Rivals:** The Traveler (chaos vs. paths); Varnek Karn (life vs. death).
- **Misc Lore:** Pre-literate storm power. Cult of the First Drum. Witch’s tool: *Storm-Drum* (shakes enemies, calls weather).
