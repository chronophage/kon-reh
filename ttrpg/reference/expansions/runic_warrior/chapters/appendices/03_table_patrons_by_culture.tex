\subsection{Note on Syncretic Worship}

Many Patrons are worshipped under different names across cultures. A devotee of the Oath of Flame \& Light in Viterra may call upon Seint Michel; in Aeler, the same Patron is the Everflame. These are not separate entities—they are the same covenant, adapted to local custom. The table above reflects the name and form most common to each culture, not a limitation on where a Patron may be found.

The thread held. The water carried. That is not a Rite. That is grace.
