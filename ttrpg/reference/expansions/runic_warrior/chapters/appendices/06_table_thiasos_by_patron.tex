\subsection*{Thiasos Types by Category}

\subsubsection*{Living Thiasos}
Creatures that breathe, eat, and bleed. They can act independently, accept Shared Burden (Fatigue transfer), and have their own Harm tracks. They can be killed.

\subsubsection*{Sentient Thiasos}
Objects that speak, remember, and witness. They cannot act independently, cannot accept Fatigue, but cannot be killed (only damaged or destroyed). They serve as both Thiasos and Codex.

\subsubsection*{Spirit Thiasos}
Incorporeal entities that witness, remember, and sometimes speak. They cannot be harmed by mundane weapons. They can pass through walls and observe without being seen. They have no physical presence but count as witnesses for all Rites.

\subsection*{Choosing a Thiasos}

When building a Runic Warrior, choose one Thiasos from your Patron's list, or work with your GM to design a custom Thiasos appropriate to your Patron and culture. A Thiasos should reflect the Runekeeper's covenant and complement their combat style.
