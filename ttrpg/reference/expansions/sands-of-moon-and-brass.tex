\begin{center}
\textbf{A culture-focused module for desert trade, nomad law, star-path navigation, and qanat politics by the caravanserai folk of the Galaninan Great Erg, Fharan Marches, and Ashaani Provinces. Built to slot into Caravans, Wilderness, Sea, Political Intrigue, and Psionics using core Fate's Edge procedures (Position/DV, SB, clocks, Strings, Favor/Leverage/Exposure).}
\end{center}

\section*{Design Goals}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Etiquette with teeth.} Water first, lamp-lit contracts, and title respect shape play as much as blades.
\item \textbf{Caravan--nomad parity.} Trade routes, tribal law, and oasis politics are equally supported.
\item \textbf{Seamless integration.} Uses existing market rules, faction clocks, and currencies---no new dice math.
\item \textbf{Low bookkeeping.} Caravan, tribe, season wheel, and 2--3 visible clocks per scenario.
\end{itemize}

\section*{Quickstart (2 minutes)}
\begin{enumerate}
\item Make a Caravan/Tribe Sheet (\S1) and a Mount/Camel Sheet (\S2). Pick 1--2 Strings (water-share tablet, lamp-oath seal, wind-tower charter) and 3--4 Tags.
\item Mark the Season Wheel (\S3) and current Theaters (erg, marches, provinces). Start Standing [6], Feud [4], Exposure [6].
\item Pick an opening Score Type (\S4): Desert Caravan, Oasis Siege, Qanat Sabotage, or Council Moot.
\item Frame the approach, set Position/DV from tags/venue, play. On any 1, GM spends from Sands \& Brass SB (\S6.4).
\end{enumerate}

\section{Score Types \& Procedures}

Pick approach (Deceit $\bullet$ Speed $\bullet$ Shock $\bullet$ Parley) $\rightarrow$ set Position/DV from venue/tags $\rightarrow$ roll.

\subsection{Desert Caravan (Objective: Profit / Passage)}

\textbf{Clocks:} Alarm [4--6], Trade [6--8], Mount Damage [4], Blood-Price [4].

\textbf{Entry:} sand gate, hidden wadi, qanat shortcut, market ruse.

\textbf{On 1s:} GM spends SB $\rightarrow$ sandstorm warning, hidden quicksand, rival caravan ambush, guide betrayal.

\textbf{Resolution:} When Trade fills, choose coin or Strings (trade charter, seasonal tithe, guide contract). If Blood-Price fills, mark Feud +1 or pay blood-silver (Favor loss or obligation clock).

\subsection{Oasis Siege (Objective: Water / Tribute)}

\textbf{Clocks:} Thirst [4], Defense [6], Sentries [4], Terms [6].

\textbf{Position tweaks:} Water-Efficient/Heat-Ready help; sandstorms/hail hurt.

\textbf{On 1s:} qanat gets blocked, arrows from palm grove, sand-burst collapses wall.

\subsection{Qanat Sabotage (Objective: Flood / Blockade)}

Use Market from Caravans/Wilderness; add desert perks:
\begin{itemize}
\item Water-Share Tablets count as String once/session in desert venues.
\item Poet's Drum can convert Audience: Warm $\rightarrow$ Favor (narrow) once/score with a verse.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Council Moot (Objective: Law / Settlement)}

\textbf{Venue:} circle of stones, winter pavilion, qanat meeting hall.

\textbf{Moves:} Oath-Swear (commit under penalty), Wager Wyrd (ordeal by feat), Blood-Silver (compensation roll), Witness the Verse (Poet stakes a truth).

\textbf{Outcomes:} resolve Feud, write Oath to ledger (acts as durable String), assign seasonal water rights or trade route.

\section{Etiquette, Blood-Silver, \& Standing}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Etiquette (mechanics):} Water first grants Position +1; lamp-lit contracts add +1 Effect; titles before names improve Audience tags.
\item \textbf{Blood-Silver (settlement):} Pay with coin, hostage-string, or deed. Roll Petition/Broker vs DV 2--4 (standing, witnesses, hurt). On hit, reduce Feud --2; on partial, --1; on miss, counter-oath demanded.
\item \textbf{Boasts \& Verses:} A public boast creates Audience: Expectant; fulfill it to gain Standing +1, fail and mark Exposure or Feud.
\end{itemize}

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Khamsin/Sandstorm:} sight Position --1; Sand-Sure cancels. On 1, Pursuit +1 (lost bearings).
\item \textbf{Flash Flood:} ranged actions --1 die; on 1, Condition +1 (saddle/gear).
\item \textbf{Ward Storm:} Navigate DV +1; on 1, choose Delay (Distance stalls) or Sand-Blast (Condition +1).
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Qanat Navigation \& Hazards}

Treat as Cross Hazard (Body+Tactics/Craft) DV 3--5; Qanat-Swimmer/Sand-Sure grant Position +1. On 1, Tunnel Collapse or Water Breach.

\subsection{Sands \& Brass SB (GM menu)}
\begin{itemize}
\item Hidden Quicksand: sudden sink; Mount Damage +1 unless Position was high.
\item Sandstorm Warning: dust wall rises; Pursuit +1 and Alarm +1.
\item Guide Betrayal: lose Position; find new path or fight lost.
\item Witness at Dune: a rival caravan sees; Exposure +1 (foreign) or Feud +1 (desert).
\item Verse Twisted: rumor flips an Audience tag against you.
\end{itemize}

\section{Desert Culture Tools (portrayal guidance)}
\begin{itemize}
\item Emphasize law and reciprocity (oaths, blood-silver, witness) over caricature.
\item Show plural livelihoods: traders, guides, water-keepers, poets, warriors---not only raiders.
\item Let women/elders hold seats and steer deals; avoid monolith tropes.
\item Lean into seasonality and council assemblies as civic life.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Etiquette Hooks (once/scene in desert venues):} offer water first; name your grandfather's line; recite a verse---each can grant Position +1 in parley.

\section{Example of Play (short)}

\textbf{Setup:} Dromedary with Sand-Sure, Night-Runner, Falcon-Eye. Caravan Standing 3/6, Feud 1/4 (with Fharan Nomads). Score: Desert Caravan to Bir al-Qamar (Trade).

\textbf{Approach:} Parley-then-Speed. Speaker petitions for priority passage (DV 3). Partial $\rightarrow$ Position stays Controlled, GM spends 1 SB.

\textbf{Action:} Guide triggers Navigate at night. Night-Runner drops DV to 2. Hit $\rightarrow$ Trade +2; a 1 shows $\rightarrow$ GM spends Sandstorm Warning (Alarm +1, Pursuit +1).

\textbf{Twist:} Flash Flood rolls in. Sand-Sure cancels Position penalty. Water-Keeper finds a Guide-Stone line: Navigate gains Position +1; Pursuit --1.

\textbf{Close:} Trade fills. Caravan chooses a seasonal guide contract String instead of coin. Blood-Price at 2/4; they pledge blood-silver at winter council to avoid Feud tick.

\section{Cross-Peoples Integration (quick rules)}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Etiquette Equivalence:} Any culture's etiquette hook can substitute in mixed venues if a local sponsor vouches; treat as Position +0 (not +1) unless the PCs hold a relevant String.
\item \textbf{Strings as Passports:} A String earned in one sphere (e.g., water-share tablet) lowers DV or raises Position in another when fictionally relevant (e.g., purchasing feed at a Sidhi quay).
\item \textbf{Markets:} Map appetites across peoples—Fhara (Relief/Ritual), Sidhi (Harbor/Luxury), Ashaani (Official/Relief), Pereshi (Artisan/Scholarly), Kuvani (Travel/Beast). Apply +1 Position when selling into a matching appetite with a native broker.
\item \textbf{Followers:} When recruiting followers from these peoples, give one culture tag that can be spent once/leg for Position +1 (e.g., Water-Finder, Pilot, Procession Marshal, Archive Clerk, Remount Handler).
\end{itemize}

\section{GM Portrayal Notes (keep it plural)}
\begin{itemize}
\item Represent multiple livelihoods within each people (clerks and camel-drivers; pilots and street brokers; gardeners and charter-makers; riders and lore-keepers).
\item Use oaths, courts, and hospitality to show social fabric; let etiquette shift mechanics, not caricature.
\item Ask players what their characters learned from hosts; bring that forward as advantage tags and Strings.
\item When in doubt, lean on law and reciprocity over spectacle.
\end{itemize}
