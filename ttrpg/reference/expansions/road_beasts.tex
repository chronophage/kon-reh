%------------------- How to Use ------------------------------------
\section*{How to Use This Manual}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Monsters are built from \kw{Harm levels} (1--3) and optional \kw{clocks} (4--8 segments). No HP math.
    \item Each entry includes \kw{Danger}, \kw{Harm}, \kw{Clocks}, \kw{Tags}, and a \kw{Fate's Edge twist}.
    \item The \kw{Lazy GM Table} (p.~4) lets you roll any adversary in 10 seconds.
    \item \kw{Character Inspiration} (p.~5): draw two playing cards to generate a random concept + complication.
\end{itemize}

%------------------- Character Inspiration -------------------------
\section*{Character Inspiration: Two Cards}
\textit{At character creation or when introducing a new NPC, draw two playing cards (suit + rank). Combine a \textbf{Concept} (Heart/Diamond) with a \textbf{Complication} (Spade/Club). No jokers.}

\begin{tabular}{@{}p{0.22\textwidth}p{0.22\textwidth}p{0.22\textwidth}p{0.22\textwidth}}
\toprule
\multicolumn{2}{c}{\textbf{Concepts (Heart or Diamond)}} &
\multicolumn{2}{c}{\textbf{Complications (Spade or Club)}} \\
\midrule
\emph{Rank} & \emph{Concept} & \emph{Rank} & \emph{Complication} \\
2--3 & Exiled noble & 2--3 & Hunted by a rival \\
4--5 & Grizzled scout & 4--5 & Carries a cursed heirloom \\
6--7 & Street-wise fixer & 6--7 & Owe a favor to a crime lord \\
8--9 & Failed scholar & 8--9 & Secretly ill (clock [4]) \\
10--J & Faith healer & 10--J & Witness to a forbidden rite \\
Q--K & Retired soldier & Q--K & Spouse missing (search clock) \\
A & Last of a bloodline & A & Marked by a spirit (visible) \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}

\textit{Then roll or pick a third card for a \textbf{Goal} (any suit):}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Red (Heart/Diamond):} Protect someone / find a treasure.
    \item \textbf{Black (Spade/Club):} destroy a rival / avenge an insult.
    \item \textbf{Ace:} discover the truth about [a place, death, or omen].
\end{itemize}

\textit{Example:} 6♦ (Grizzled scout) + 8♣ (Secretly ill) + Q♠ (avenge an insult) = a sick tracker who wants revenge on the poacher who killed her mentor.

%------------------- Final Notes -----------------------------------
\section*{Final Notes for the Lazy GM}
\begin{itemize}
    \item You never need to invent a monster from scratch. Use the table above and rename.
    \item For a \textbf{leader-type}, add a clock that summons minions. For a \textbf{swarm}, treat as a zone hazard (test to avoid Harm each turn).
    \item Fate's Edge monsters are not sacks of HP. They have \textbf{twists} -- use them to create choices, not just fights.
    \item When a player says ``I know this creature,'' let them roll Lore. On success, they invent one of its tags (subject to your veto).
\end{itemize}
