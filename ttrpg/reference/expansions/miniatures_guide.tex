\section{Core Setup}
Declare \textbf{square} or \textbf{hex} at setup. Both use the same rules, adapted:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Base Sizes:} Small (1 cell), Medium (2×1), Large (2×2), Huge (3×3). Large+ project ZoC from all edge cells.
  \item \textbf{Actions:} Move and Act (in either order). Core resolution remains unchanged.
  \item \textbf{Speed:} $5 + \text{Body}$ cells. Difficult terrain costs +1 per cell.
\end{itemize}

\section{Zone of Control (ZOC)}
You control the cells adjacent to your base:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Squares:} 4 orthogonals (default) or 8 including diagonals (declare at setup).
  \item \textbf{Hexes:} All 6 adjacent hexes.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{ZOC Rules}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Entering:} You may enter enemy ZoC, but your movement \textbf{immediately ends}. You are \textbf{Engaged}.
  \item \textbf{Passing Through:} \textbf{Prohibited.} You cannot move through any enemy ZoC.
  \item \textbf{Leaving:} Take the \textbf{Disengage} action (\DV{} $4 + \text{enemy's Tier}$) or spend \textbf{1 \Boon} to leave automatically. On failure, remain Engaged.
  \item \textbf{Multiple ZoCs:} Disengage \DV{} $+1$ per additional enemy.
  \item \textbf{Push-Through:} Force through ZoC without Disengaging: roll $\text{Body} + \text{Athletics}$ vs.\ \DV{} $2 + \text{enemy's Tier}$. Choose one cost:
    \begin{itemize}
      \item Reduce Position by one step (min \textbf{Desperate}) on next action this turn, \textbf{or}
      \item $-1$ die on next roll, \textbf{or}
      \item Grant enemy immediate opportunity attack at $+1$ die.
    \end{itemize}
    On failure, stop in their ZoC (Engaged). \textit{Tier IV+: Spend 1 \Boon{} to ignore ZoC for one Move.}
\end{itemize}

\section{Facing and Combat}
Choose facing after moving. Arcs determine bonuses:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Squares:} Front = 3 cells ahead; Sides = 2 flanks; Rear = 3 back.
  \item \textbf{Hexes:} Front = 3 hexes ahead; Sides = 2 lateral; Rear = 1 back.
\end{itemize}

\paragraph{Attack Bonuses}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Flank:} Allies on opposite arcs grant each other $+1$ die (max $+3$ total assistance).
  \item \textbf{Rear:} $+1$ die and $+1$ Effect.
  \item \textbf{Guarded Strike:} When an enemy leaves your ZoC, make an immediate attack at $-1$ die (or worse Position).
\end{itemize}

\paragraph{Special Actions}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Shove:} Move target 1 cell on hit. Cannot push through ZoC; destination controllers may strike.
  \item \textbf{Brace:} Until next turn, resist Shoves at $-1$ die and extend ZoC by $+1$ ring for opportunity attacks only.
  \item \textbf{Take Cover:} Move behind terrain to improve Position vs.\ ranged (e.g., \textbf{Desperate} $\rightarrow$ \textbf{Controlled}).
\end{itemize}

\section{Magic and [TAGS]}\label{sec:magic}
Spells use \Tags{} that interact with grid position:

\begin{itemize}
  \item \Tag{WARD}: Blocks passage. Test \DV{} = Spirit or Cap to cross; on hit, add \DV{} to Leash/Exit Tally.
  \item \Tag{CONJURE}: Creates obstacles (Integrity 2/4/6) that block LoS and ZoC like terrain.
  \item \Tag{TRANSPORT}: Requires clear LoS and destination outside enemy ZoC (or Push-through applies).
  \item \Tag{PASSAGE}: Allies gain Position/Effect when moving through declared routes.
  \item \Tag{AREA}: Affects a zone (radius in cells). Center must be within range; may hit friendly ZoCs.
\end{itemize}

\paragraph{Casting in Melee}
Casting while Engaged worsens Position by one step unless the spell is \Tag{INSTANT} or you have a relevant Talent.

\paragraph{Rituals}
Require clear space (no enemy ZoC) and visible Symbols. Interrupted rituals fail or require immediate test (\DV{} 4).

\section{SRD Integration}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{\Boons:} Spend 1 to Disengage automatically, ignore ZoC for one step, or change facing freely.
  \item \textbf{\Boons:} Spend 2 for \textbf{Heroic Rush}: move full Speed ignoring ZoC, but end Engaged with one enemy of choice.
  \item \textbf{\DV{} Reference:} Disengage 4+Tier; Shove 4; Tackle 4–6; Balance (elevation) 3–5.
  \item \textbf{Cover:} Light ($-1$ die to attackers); Heavy ($-2$ dice, no Rear bonuses).
\end{itemize}

\section{Quick Reference}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Entering ZoC ends movement; passing through is prohibited.
  \item Disengage: Action (\DV{} $4+\text{Tier}$) or 1 \Boon{}. Push-through: Test vs.\ \DV{} $2+\text{Tier}$ with cost.
  \item Flank: $+1$ die; Rear: $+1$ die and Effect.
  \item Casting in ZoC: Position $-1$ unless \Tag{INSTANT}.
  \item \Tag{WARD} blocks movement; \Tag{CONJURE} creates terrain; \Tag{TRANSPORT} obeys ZoC.
\end{itemize}

\bigskip
\noindent\textit{Design: Position decides fights. ZOCs shape lanes, facing rewards planning, and \Boons{} let heroes break the geometry exactly when the story demands.}
