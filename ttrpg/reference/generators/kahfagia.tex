\subsection*{Regional Mood: The Lantern's Vigil & The Spider's Web}

Power is visible here ‚ąí‚ąí‚ąí literally. Every night, a thousand lamps burn along the channels, each a statement of control. To dim a light is to cede ground. To move a beacon is to declare war. Commodores sit in their mirror‚ąítowers, watching reflections, while tide‚ąítimers tick down to the next auction, the next inspection, the next quiet burial of a reputation.

But beneath the visible power, another war is fought in whispers. The Lethai‚ąíar agents of Inaea have spent a generation infiltrating the oligarchic families. A young heir who suddenly displays uncanny negotiating skill may not be talented ‚ąí‚ąí‚ąí they may be \emph{woven}. A Mirror‚ąíKeeper whose beacons never fail may be receiving guidance from a source that does not appear on any chart. The Spider's web is patient. It does not break the thalassocracy; it \emph{guides} it.

\subsection*{The Oligarchic Houses (Shadow Council)}
Ňāabel{sec:kahfagia‚ąíhouses}

The true rulers of Kahfagia are not the Admiralty alone, but the \textbf{Shadow Council} of seven oligarchic families. Each controls a monopoly (spice, salt, silk, timber, pitch, shipbuilding, or intelligence). Each maintains its own private navy and network of spies. Each vies for the empty seat left by the family that the Lethai‚ąíar have already \emph{woven} into silence.

\begin{itemize}
  ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{House al‚ąíKassam (Spice):} Oldest, proudest, most vulnerable. The Lethai‚ąíar have already replaced the family steward.
  ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{House Carrasco (Salt):} Ruthless pragmatists; they suspect infiltration but cannot prove it.
  ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{House Mirat (Silk):} Secret allies of the Lethai‚ąíar ‚ąí‚ąí‚ąí they trade webs for influence.
  ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{House del Puerto (Shipbuilding):} Neutral; they build for anyone who pays.
  ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{House Henares (Timber):} Deeply weighed down by Admiralty claims; the Lethai‚ąíar are slowly gathering leverage.
  ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{House de la Vela (Intelligence):} The Admiralty's own spy network ‚ąí‚ąí‚ąí and already compromised.
  ‚ĄĻtem \textbf{The Empty Chair:} Once held by House Morat, now vacant. The other houses whisper that the Morats were not destroyed ‚ąí‚ąí‚ąí they were \emph{rewoven}.
\end{itemize}
