# Fate's Edge: Complete Campaign Framework

## Overview
This document provides a comprehensive party setup that can playtest all available Fate's Edge modules, starting at XP 0 with a balanced group of characters designed to engage with the full breadth of the system's mechanics and thematic elements.

## Campaign Integration Hooks

### Cross-Module Connections
1. **The Blockade War** (Amaranthine) affects **Caravan Trade** (Silk Road) routes
2. **Political Intrigue** in cities connects to **Dragon Cults** seeking patronage
3. **Psionic Research** in academic centers ties to **Horror Elements** in forbidden studies
4. **Desert Politics** (Sands of Moon and Brass) influence **Caravan Economics**
5. **Linn Oath-Bonds** create **Political Alliances** in coastal regions
6. **Dragon Hoards** contain artifacts from **Ancient Civilizations** featured in all modules

### Progression Path
1. **Tier I** (0-40 XP): Establish core group dynamics, local reputation
2. **Tier II** (41-90 XP): Regional influence, access to major factions
3. **Tier III** (91-150 XP): National recognition, dragon encounters, major political roles
4. **Tier IV** (151-220 XP): Cross-domain operations, patron-level interactions
5. **Tier V** (221+ XP): Mythic status, world-changing decisions

## Session Zero Agenda

### 60-Minute Framework
1. **Safety Tools** (10 minutes): Lines/Veils, X-Card protocol, consent for horror elements
2. **Character Integration** (15 minutes): Review bonds, complications, establish starting location
3. **Campaign Scope** (10 minutes): Choose primary module to begin, identify cross-module hooks
4. **Initial Situation** (15 minutes): Frame opening scenario using integrated setup elements
5. **Mechanics Overview** (10 minutes): Quick review of key systems (clocks, SB economy, magic)

## Starting Scenario: "The Convergence Storm"

### Premise
A rare celestial alignment is causing magical disturbances across multiple domains. Storms rage in the Amaranthine Sea, political upheaval shakes city-states, and ancient powers stir in their lairs. The party's diverse skills make them uniquely positioned to investigate and respond.

### Setup Elements
- **Amaranthine Sea:** Storm pressure affecting all maritime travel
- **Political Intrigue:** Emergency councils called in major cities
- **Wilderness:** Weather anomalies disrupting normal travel patterns
- **Psionics:** Mental resonance effects amplifying psychic phenomena
- **Dragon's Lair:** Ancient wyrm awakening responds to celestial forces

### Opening Hooks
1. Kestra's storm raven familiar becomes agitated and speaks of "the convergence"
2. Thane receives an honor-duel challenge from a rival clan affected by the storms
3. Seris detects a psychic disturbance affecting multiple minds across regions
4. Daro's lantern shows unusual omens pointing to a specific geographic convergence
5. Renn's caravan route reports supernatural weather blocking traditional paths

This party setup provides balanced coverage of all major mechanical systems while maintaining narrative coherence across modules. Each character brings unique strengths that complement others, and the integrated complications ensure ongoing story threads that can develop through any module's mechanics.
