\subsection{The Confessor Beneath the Bell (Burden \& Exchange)}

\textit{Lore.} The Confessor waits in places where guilt gathers---under church spires, in confessionals long abandoned, in the echo of unspoken sins. He does not judge, nor absolve. He \emph{takes}. Each confession is a transaction: your burden for his silence, your pain for his weight. Those who serve him walk with bowed heads, their mouths stitched by invisible threads, ringing faintly when they speak. To follow the Confessor is to understand that sin does not vanish---it migrates, carried from one soul to another until the bell tolls and the weight becomes unbearable.

\begin{quote}
``Speak, and be freed. But understand---what you cast off, I will bear. And what I bear, you will one day inherit.''
\end{quote}

\subsubsection*{Rites of the Confessor}

\paragraph{Echo of Sin (Low, 4 XP)} \hfill \\
\emph{Scene; Near; Yes.} \\
\textbf{Materials:} A hollow vessel or the ringing of a bell. \\
\textbf{Effect:} Absorb 1 Story Beat (Hearts or Diamonds) from an ally or NPC within Near range. Convert it into \textbf{+1 Obligation}. You know the emotional nature of the sin but not its source. \\
\textbf{Push It:} Absorb 2 SB at once; mark \textbf{+1 Fatigue} as your body trembles with resonance. \\
\emph{Requires: Familiar (\textit{Invoke:} 1 Boon).}

\paragraph{Burden Transference (Low, 5 XP)} \hfill \\
\emph{Scene; Near; No.} \\
\textbf{Materials:} A red cord or shared drink. \\
\textbf{Effect:} Transfer one Condition, Fatigue, or 1 SB from another target to yourself. You may attempt to reduce their Harm by 1 level; if successful, mark 1 Fatigue. \\
\textbf{Push It:} Split the burden among two allies; mark \textbf{1 SB (Hearts)}. \\
\emph{Requires: Familiar (\textit{Invoke:} 1 Boon).}

\paragraph{Absolution by Ash (Low, 4 XP)} \hfill \\
\emph{Scene; Touch; Yes (sustain by prayer).} \\
\textbf{Materials:} A pinch of consecrated ash or soot from a burned confession. \\
\textbf{Effect:} Lay hands upon a penitent and whisper a truth they cannot bear. Reduce their \textbf{Corruption by 1}. The sin transfers to you as \textit{Burdened Heart}: mark 1 SB (Hearts) or \textbf{+1 Obligation}. \\
\textbf{Push It:} Cleanse multiple targets within Close range (e.g., after a battle); each additional soul adds \textbf{+1 Obligation}. \\
\emph{Requires: Familiar (\textit{Invoke:} 1 Boon).}

\paragraph{Sin-Eater's Pact (Standard, 7 XP)} \hfill \\
\emph{Scene; Touch; No.} \\
\textbf{Materials:} Shared confession whispered into an object or tattooed sigil. \\
\textbf{Effect:} Create a bond between you and the confessor; you may suffer 1 Fatigue to grant \textbf{+2 dice} to their Resolve test. If they fail, you suffer 1 Harm instead of them. \\
\textbf{Push It:} You may take both Harm and a Condition; mark \textbf{1 SB (Clubs)}. \\
\emph{Requires: Familiar + Codex (\textit{Invoke:} 1 Boon).}

\paragraph{Echoed Voice (Standard, 8 XP)} \hfill \\
\emph{Scene; Self; No.} \\
\textbf{Materials:} A small handbell or resonant surface. \\
\textbf{Effect:} Once/scene, replay the echo of a confession you have absorbed. Compel a target who shares its nature (Guilt, Regret, Betrayal) to act or reveal truth; DV = your Presence. \\
\textbf{Push It:} The echo manifests physically; mark \textbf{+1 Obligation} and \textbf{1 SB (Diamonds)}. \\
\emph{Requires: Familiar + Codex (\textit{Invoke:} 1 Boon).}

\paragraph{Bearing of the Flame (Standard, 7 XP)} \hfill \\
\emph{Scene; Near; No.} \\
\textbf{Materials:} A candle lit from a funeral pyre or sanctified brazier. \\
\textbf{Effect:} Kindle the fire of confession into yourself. Choose:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Purge:} Reduce your own \textbf{Corruption by 1} and clear 1 SB (Hearts). Allies within Near gain \textbf{+1 die} to their next \textit{Resolve} roll.
  \item \textbf{Transmute:} Ignite a consecrated symbol (bell, candle, blade) to carry the burden. The object gains the \texttt{[CONFESSION]} tag until session's end.
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Push It:} Purge two points of Corruption, but the GM may introduce a new Complication Clock (Fire, Revelation, or Repentance). \\
\emph{Requires: Familiar + Codex (\textit{Invoke:} 1 Boon).}

\paragraph{Bearing the Weight (High, 11 XP)} \hfill \\
\emph{Scene; Self; No.} \\
\textbf{Materials:} Chains, bells, or weighted relics. \\
\textbf{Effect:} Take upon yourself the burdens of a group (up to Tier + Presence). Each member removes one Condition, transferring it to you as 1 Fatigue or 1 Harm. You gain \textbf{+1 Effect} when acting to protect them. \\
\textbf{Push It:} Extend to all allies in scene; mark \textbf{+2 Obligation}. \\
\emph{Requires: Familiar + Codex + Tier III (\textit{Invoke:} 2 Boons).} \\
\emph{Obligation:} 6 segments.

\paragraph{Atonement's Toll (High, 13 XP)} \hfill \\
\emph{Scene; Near; No.} \\
\textbf{Materials:} A great bell or echoing space. \\
\textbf{Effect:} Release all burdens at once: clear all absorbed SB and Obligation. Every affected ally rolls Resolve (DV 4) or relives their confessed sin as hallucination. \\
\textbf{Push It:} Bind a spirit or soul within the toll; mark \textbf{1 SB (Spades)} and \textbf{+1 Obligation} permanently. \\
\emph{Requires: Familiar + Codex + Tier III (\textit{Invoke:} 2 Boons).} \\
\emph{Obligation:} 7 segments.

\paragraph{Sacrament of Shared Suffering (High, 12 XP)} \hfill \\
\emph{Scene; Zone; No.} \\
\textbf{Materials:} A circle of confessionals, chains linking each seat, the tears of seven penitents. \\
\textbf{Effect:} All present must confess their darkest secret or suffer \textbf{-2 dice} to all rolls. Shared sins grant \textbf{+1 die} to aid each other; conflicting sins let the GM spend SB to escalate conflicts. \\
\textbf{Push It:} Confessions become magically binding oaths; breakers suffer automatic Harm 2; mark \textbf{+2 Obligation}. \\
\emph{Requires: Familiar + Codex + Tier III (\textit{Invoke:} 2 Boons).} \\
\emph{Obligation:} 7 segments.

\paragraph{The Weight of Ages (High, 14 XP)} \hfill \\
\emph{Extended; Touch; No.} \\
\textbf{Materials:} An ancient bell that has tolled for every execution in a kingdom, a chain forged from confession nails, the ashes of a martyr who died for silence. \\
\textbf{Effect:} Transfer accumulated guilt of generations to a single target. Target must make Spirit+Resolve (DV 5) or become overwhelmed by ancestral shame, suffering automatic Harm 2. \\
\textbf{Push It:} The target's guilt infects their bloodline; mark \textbf{3 SB (Spades)}. \\
\emph{Requires: Familiar + Codex + Tier III (\textit{Invoke:} 2 Boons).} \\
\emph{Obligation:} 8 segments.

\subsubsection*{Cantors \& Cults of the Confessor}

\textbf{The Cantor's Song.} A Cantor of the Confessor sings in bells, not words -- their voice strikes the air like a clapper on bronze. They perform \textit{intonations of release}, melodies that invite confession from unwilling throats and then swallow it whole. Their songs are used by torturers who wish to be merciful, and by priests who cannot bear the weight of what they hear.

\textbf{The Cult of the Silent Bell.} The Confessor's cults gather in places where guilt is already thick: courthouses after sentencing, battlefields the morning after, deathbeds where the dying strain to speak. They wear grey hoods and carry small handbells that they ring exactly once at the start of a confession, and once when the burden has been transferred. Their creed is simple: \textit{"We do not forgive. We carry. When we can no longer carry, we pass the load to the one beneath the bell."}

\subsubsection*{Witchcraft of the Confessor (Threshold / Craft)}

A witch who walks with the Confessor is a \textit{bell‑wright} or \textit{burden‑smith} -- a crafter of thresholds that listen and containers that hold. Their work is often indistinguishable from craft: forging a bell that echoes only the unspoken, carving a confessional that remembers every whisper, stitching a cord that ties sin to stone.

\textbf{The Witch's Tool: The Listening Chisel.} A cold‑iron chisel that never dulls. When used to carve a confession into a stone or a wooden beam, that surface will "remember" the words for one year and one day. Once per session, you may strike the chisel against a threshold (a door, a bridge, a city gate) and ask the GM one question about a secret confession made near it.

\textbf{A Hedge Gift: Bell of Borrowed Silence (2 XP).} Once per scene, you may tap a small handbell and absorb one mundane sound (a scream, a lie, a name spoken in anger) into the bell's resonance. For the remainder of the scene, you may release that sound as a distraction or a weapon (\emph{+1 die to Intimidation or Deception}).
