\subsection{Mab, Queen of Courts (Glamour \& Bargain)}
\textit{Lore.} Mab rules not from throne or blade, but from dance and debt. She is the smile that binds, the jest that ensnares, the hostess who makes guests complicit in her game. To speak in her Court is to pay; to receive her token is to owe.  

Where others rule by force, Mab rules by etiquette, glamour, and the hidden hook in every gift. Her followers thrive on charm, wit, and story, spreading webs of bargains too subtle to escape. The Cantorâ€™s Path sings her name most sweetly, for every verse carries a price.  

\begin{quote}
``Every laugh is a promise. Every promise is a debt. Every debt belongs to Mab.''
\end{quote}

\paragraph*{Domain Focus}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Fae Glamour:} Illusions, enchantments, faerie magic
\item \textbf{Social Bargains:} Oaths, promises, debt economy
\item \textbf{Courtly Intrigue:} Etiquette, wordplay, social games
\item \textbf{Trickster Arts:} Mischief, cleverness, ironic twists
\end{itemize}

\paragraph*{Rite of the Trickster's Bargain (Low, 4 XP)} 
\emph{Scene; Near; Standard Push}\\
\textbf{Materials:} Token freely given (flower, coin, ribbon).\\
\textbf{Effect:} Offer fae bargain. Target accepts (+1 die to both for terms) or refuses (target marks 1 Stress). 4-segment \emph{Bargain's Edge} clock.\\
\textbf{Push It:} Betrayal inflicts Harm 1 (Stress); start \emph{Oathbreaker} [4] clock.

\paragraph*{Rite of Courtly Guise [VEIL] (Low, 4 XP)} 
\emph{Scene; Self; Standard Push}\\
\textbf{Materials:} Sprig or silver thread worn visibly.\\
\textbf{Effect:} Subtle glamour grants +1 die to social rolls in refined settings. You appear as expected rank/guest.\\
\textbf{Push It:} Mask one personal tell; first probing question generates 1 SB (Hearts).

\paragraph*{Rite of Token of Favor (Low, 5 XP)} 
\emph{Scene; Near; Standard Push}\\
\textbf{Materials:} Ribbon, ring, or charm bestowed ritually.\\
\textbf{Effect:} Ally gains +1 die to social actions before witnesses; you gain +1 Effect aiding them. 6-segment \emph{Favor's Weight} clock.\\
\textbf{Push It:} Token silences hecklers for one exchange; you mark +1 Exposure.

\paragraph*{Rite of Mirror of Motives [REVEAL] (Standard, 8 XP)} 
\emph{Action; Near; Standard Push}\\
\textbf{Materials:} Polished shard or hand mirror.\\
\textbf{Effect:} Learn target's immediate social aim; gain +1 die exploiting it this scene. Creates 4-segment \emph{Insight} clock.\\
\textbf{Push It:} Surface concealed insult; generate 1 SB (Hearts) against target.

\paragraph*{Rite of the Price Agreed [OATH] (Standard, 9 XP)} 
\emph{Scene; Near; Standard Push}\\
\textbf{Materials:} Equal tokens exchanged ritually.\\
\textbf{Effect:} Bind bargain. Breach forces 1 SB (Hearts/Diamonds) and reputation damage. 8-segment \emph{Bargain Binding} clock.\\
\textbf{Push It:} Add minor boon (+1 die once); you suffer 1 SB if breached.

\paragraph*{Rite of Sovereign Glamour [VEIL][REVEAL] (High, 13 XP)} 
\emph{Ritual; Zone; High Push}\\
\textbf{Materials:} Circle of silk or green felt, courtly music.\\
\textbf{Effect:} Establish fae court. Allies +1 die to social rolls; blunt threats -1 die. Once strip illusion. 10-segment \emph{Court Dominion} clock.\\
\textbf{Push It:} Impose Court Law (e.g., "no steel"); violators suffer 2 SB.\\
\textbf{Obligation:} 7 segments.

\paragraph*{Rite of the Eternal Masquerade [WARD][BIND] (High, 14 XP)} 
\emph{Extended; Large Zone; High Push}\\
\textbf{Materials:} Masks for all participants, enchanted music.\\
\textbf{Effect:} Create lasting glamoured court. All social magic gains +1 Effect; outsiders suffer -1 die to perceive truths here.\\
\textbf{Push It:} Masquerade persists between scenes; start \emph{Glamour Maintenance} [8] clock.\\
\textbf{Obligation:} 8 segments.

\paragraph*{Playstyle Notes}
Mab excels in social manipulation and fae-style bargaining. Followers become masters of subtle magic, binding others through oaths and glamour. The corruption progression leads toward becoming fae-touched, gaining power over truth and perception at the cost of mortal constraints. Ideal for players who enjoy social intrigue, wordplay, and characters who prefer cleverness over brute force.
