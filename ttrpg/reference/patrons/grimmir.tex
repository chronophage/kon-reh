\subsubsection{Grimmir, the Wild Speaker (Primal Wisdom \& Seasonal Cycles)}
\textit{Lore.} Grimmir walks the threshold between cultivated lands and untamed wilderness, embodying the ancient pact between humanity and the natural world. Neither wholly beast nor entirely human, Grimmir speaks in root and stone, seasons and sap. Those who walk his path gain insight into nature's patterns, the tongues of leaf and fang, and mastery over the primal forces that shape the wild placesâ€”yet must choose which voice to trust: the cultivated mind or the feral heart.

\begin{quote}
``In the space between planted row and forest edge, where the first grain met the first acorn, Grimmir waits. He speaks in the rustle of leaves, the migration of birds, and the patient growth of ancient oaks. Listen, and learn that true power comes not from dominion, but from harmony.''
\end{quote}

\noindent\textbf{Domain Focus}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Seasonal Wisdom:} cyclical patterns, timing, and natural rhythms
  \item \textbf{Verdant Speech:} communion with plant and animal life
  \item \textbf{Growth \& Decay:} life-cycles, healing, and transformation
  \item \textbf{Boundary Keeping:} guarding wild spaces and the old order
\end{itemize}

\paragraph*{Rite of the Speaking Seed (Low, 4 XP)} \emph{Scene; Zone; No.}\\
\textbf{Materials:} A seed from the local biome or soil from undisturbed earth.\\
\textbf{Effect:} Establish communion with local plant life. Gain \(+1\) die to \textit{Survival} or \textit{Investigation} when seeking information about the area. Plants in the zone provide minor assistance (parting grass, masking scent, concealing movement).\\
\textbf{Push It:} Flora actively aid for one exchange; mark 1 segment on \textbf{Primal Awareness}.

\paragraph*{Rite of the Season's Turn (Low, 5 XP)} \emph{Scene; Self; Yes.}\\
\textbf{Materials:} A token of the current season (flower, fruit, fallen leaf, budding branch).\\
\textbf{Effect:} Align with seasonal power. Choose one: \(+1\) die to actions keyed to the season (Spring/Growth; Summer/Abundance; Autumn/Harvest; Winter/Endurance) \emph{or} gain resistance to hazards of the season.\\
\textbf{Push It:} Extend the benefit to one ally; both mark \textbf{Exposure} to seasonal extremes.

\paragraph*{Rite of Verdant Tongue (Standard, 8 XP)} \emph{Scene; Zone; Yes.}\\
\textbf{Materials:} A drop of honey, a grain of pollen, or morning dew cupped in both hands.\\
\textbf{Effect:} Speak with all plant and animal life in \textit{Near}. Gain \(+2\) dice to gather information about local conditions, threats, or movements. Beasts may offer minor aid (distraction, guidance, warning).\\
\textbf{Push It:} Compel one significant creature to aid directly (Resolve DV~4) \emph{or} take a prophetic reading from an elder being; mark \textbf{+1 Obligation}.\\
\emph{Obligation:} 5 segments.

\paragraph*{Rite of the Thornveil (Standard, 7 XP)} \emph{Scene; Zone; Yes.}\\
\textbf{Materials:} A thorn, briar, or \emph{thorny branch} woven into a protective circle.\\
\textbf{Effect:} Raise a living barrier. The zone gains the \texttt{[WARD]} tag against those who would harm the natural order. Allies gain \(+1\) die to \textit{Stealth}/\textit{Defense}; aggressors suffer \(-1\) die to approach. Start a \textbf{Natural Defense [6]} clock.\\
\textbf{Push It:} The barrier endures the full scene but draws territorial spirits; mark \textbf{1~SB (Clubs)}.\\
\emph{Obligation:} 4 segments.

\paragraph*{Rite of the World's Wound (High, 13 XP)} \emph{Extended; Zone; Yes.}\\
\textbf{Materials:} A sacred grove-site, a circle of stones, an offering of blood or seed.\\
\textbf{Effect:} Heal a blighted place or quicken a damaged ecosystem. Clear taint, restore fertility, awaken dormant forces. Gain \(+2\) dice to related \textit{Survival}/\textit{Nature} actions in the region. Start an \textbf{Ecosystem Restoration [8]} clock.\\
\textbf{Push It:} The cure spreads to adjacent areas but unbalances another; mark \textbf{2~SB (Diamonds)}.\\
\emph{Obligation:} 7 segments.

\paragraph*{Rite of the Cycle's Crown (High, 14 XP)} \emph{Scene; Self/Zone; Yes.}\\
\textbf{Materials:} A circlet of woven branches, antlers, and seasonal tokens.\\
\textbf{Effect:} Become an avatar of the cycle. Choose two:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Seasonal Mastery:} \(+2\) dice to all actions keyed to the current season.
  \item \textbf{Verdant Command:} Command plant life in \textit{Near} automatically.
  \item \textbf{Cycle's Healing:} Remove one \textbf{Condition} or downgrade \textbf{Harm} by one level.
  \item \textbf{Growth's Protection:} Allies gain \(+1\) \textbf{Armor} from natural cover.
\end{itemize}
Begin a \textbf{Seasonal Avatar [6]} clock.\\
\textbf{Push It:} Extend the blessings to all present; mark \textbf{+2 Obligation} and gain \textbf{Seasonal Attunement} (see Corruption).\\
\emph{Obligation:} 8 segments.

\subsubsection*{Playstyle Notes}
Grimmir rewards harmony over conquest, cyclical thinking, and fluent communication with the living world. Expect environmental problem-solving, seasonal planning, and hard choices at the boundary of field and forest. Power grows with attunement; the price is comfort, certainty, and the ease of polite society.
