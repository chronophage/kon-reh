Kon'reh — Core Rules (Mechanics Only)

This document lists procedures, constraints, and timers only (no lore or strategy text).

1) Objective

  • Capture the opponent’s Blue. After your Blue is captured, you have five of your own turns to plant a banner (end a move) on the enemy Home Apex. If you succeed, your Blue returns (see Reforge). If you do not, you lose immediately.

2) Equipment & Setup (2-player baseline)

  • Board: 8×8 diamond (four corner apexes: your Home, enemy Home “Opposing,” and two side Sanctums).
  • Each side controls tokens by class: Blue ×1, Orange ×2, Red (several). Greens are generated during play (shared pool; see cap below).
  • Typical starting array (from your corner toward center): R1/Home Apex: Blue. R2: Orange, Orange. R3: Red, Green, Red. Remaining squares empty. (Greens on R3 are placeholders; actual Green availability still obeys global cap.)
  • Provide a shared supply of Green tokens; enforce the global cap defined in Seed.

3) Turn Structure

  • Players alternate single moves; Player 2 takes a double-move on their first turn (two different tokens).
  • One move: choose one token and execute a legal operation (slide; or for Blue, slide then one special).

4) Movement & Zone of Control (ZoC)

  • Slides are along a single straight lane (no turning within a move).
  • Pieces do not pass through occupied squares.
  • ZoC: Each token projects ZoC to its four edge-adjacent squares. You may enter enemy ZoC, but entering ZoC ends the move. Passing through ZoC is not allowed.
  • Captures (non-Blue): Red/Orange/Green capture by displacement (step onto the occupied enemy square; remove that enemy).

5) Blue (special piece) — Specials & Stagger

  • On a Blue turn you may slide, then perform exactly one special. You may never special → slide.
  • Displacement (S:D): Move 1 square along the current lane onto an adjacent enemy; remove that enemy.
  • Hop-capture (S:H): Jump over one adjacent enemy along the lane to the immediately empty square beyond; remove the jumped enemy. (Landing square must be empty.)
  • ZoC gate: If your Blue’s slide entered enemy ZoC this turn, you cannot use a special afterward.
  • Crown Stagger: If a Blue uses both distinct specials (H and D) in the same life, it becomes Rooted until your next turn (cannot move; may still be captured).

6) Twin Apex Seed (spawning Greens)

  • Condition: If your Blue ends its move on a Sanctum and the opposite Sanctum is empty, you may place one Green on that opposite Sanctum, provided the global Green cap < 6.
  • Rooted on Seed: The Blue that Seeded is Rooted until your next turn.
  • Mobilization Delay: A Blue cannot Seed on its first departure from Home in its current life.
  • Same-Sanctum ban (per life): A Blue placed on a Sanctum by Reforge may not Seed from that same Sanctum for the remainder of that Blue life.

7) Central Four (“Cross”)

  • The Cross is the 2×2 center diamond.
  • Stay cap: A Blue may end at most 3 of its own turns in the Cross per life.
  • Exclusion: After a Blue ends a turn outside the Cross, it may not re-enter the Cross for the next 2 of its own turns. (Entering and exiting in the same turn does not start exclusion.)

8) Reforge (after Blue is captured)

  • When your Blue is captured, start a personal countdown of 5 of your turns. To succeed, plant a banner by ending any of your tokens’ moves on the enemy Home Apex before the countdown reaches zero.
  • On success: Remove the runner that planted. Immediately return your Blue by one of:
    • Opposing Apex (their Home): Pay 1 Green (sacrifice).
    • Either Sanctum: No cost; this Blue may not Seed from that same Sanctum for the rest of this life.
    • Your Home Apex: No cost.
  • Refresh: A reforged Blue returns with both specials refreshed.
  • On failure: If your countdown reaches 0 without a banner plant, you lose immediately.

9) Table State Tracking (recommended but optional)

  • [CF:in x/3] — Cross stays this life.
  • [Excl:y] — Cross re-entry exclusion turns remaining.
  • [S:H] / [S:D] — Specials used this life.
  • [Rooted] — Blue cannot move until your next turn.
  • [RC n/5] — Your Reforge countdown (when your Blue was captured).
  • Shared dial for Global Green cap (0–6).
