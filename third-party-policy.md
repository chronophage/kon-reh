# Fate's Edge Third-Party Creator Policy

**Version 1.0 | June 2026**

---

## Introduction

Fate's Edge is a game built for community. The core mechanics—the dice system, Position/Effect, Boons and Story Beats, the TAGS magic system, and the character creation framework—are open for you to use, remix, and build upon. The world of the Amaranthine, its characters, its lore, and its adventures are the work of years of care. That stays close to the hearth.

This policy explains what you can do with Fate's Edge content, what you need permission for, and how to get it.

---

## Part 1: What's Open (The SRD)

The **Fate's Edge System Reference Document (SRD)** is licensed under **CC BY-NC-SA 4.0**.

### You May:

- **Use the mechanics** in your own games, supplements, and adaptations
- **Remix and adapt** the core rules to fit your vision
- **Share your work** with the community—for free—under the same license
- **Reference Fate's Edge mechanics** in actual play streams, podcasts, and videos
- **Create and sell content** that *uses* the SRD mechanics, as long as it does not incorporate extended material (see Part 2)

### You Must:

- **Credit the creator** (`Nicholas A. Gasper`) and link to the license
- **Share your adaptations** under the same CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license
- **Not use the SRD commercially** in any product that competes with or incorporates extended Fate's Edge material (see Part 2)

### You May Not:

- **Claim the mechanics as your own**—credit must be given
- **Close the license**—your adaptations must remain open
- **Use Fate's Edge trademarks** (logos, titles, character names, setting terms) without permission (see Part 4)

## Part 2: What's Closed (Extended Content)

The following are **All Rights Reserved** and require express written permission for any commercial use:

### Prohibited Without Permission:

- **Setting Lore:** The Amaranthine, its regions (Ecktoria, Acasia, Valewood, etc.), cultures, and history
- **Original Characters:** Named NPCs, Patrons, and iconic figures (e.g., Serafine of the Velvet Touch, the Gray Wanderer, Lady Isolde)
- **Adventures and Modules:** The Lantern at Dusk, Blood and Silk, Beyond Millhaven, The Brass Gate, and any published adventures
- **Proprietary Magic Systems:** Full patron entries, Invoker Rites, Cantor Songs, Runkeeper Codex content, and Summoner-specific mechanics as published (SRD magic mechanics remain open)
- **Artwork and Graphics:** All illustrations, maps, logos, and design elements
- **Faction and Organization Descriptions:** Detailed faction entries (e.g., The Vigil, the Black Banners, the Silk Vigil)
- **Region-Specific Generators:** Detailed generators and cultural mechanics as published in the Amaranthine Core
- **Original Prose and Framing Devices:** All narrative text, quotes, and in-character framing

### What This Means:

You can write a supplement that uses the Fate's Edge dice system and references "the Position/Effect mechanic" or "Boons and Story Beats." You cannot set it in the Amaranthine, use Serafine as an NPC, or publish an adventure that takes place in Silkstrand without permission.

## Part 3: Commercial Use Permission

If you want to create and sell content that uses **extended material** (setting, characters, adventures, proprietary mechanics, etc.), you need permission.

### How to Apply:

Send a request to **`[PUBLISHER_EMAIL]`** with:

1. **Your name and contact information**
2. **A description of your project** (what it is, what it includes, who it's for)
3. **Which extended material you want to use** (be specific: "the city of Silkstrand," "the patron Ikasha," "the adventure The Lantern at Dusk")
4. **How you plan to distribute and price it** (PDF, print, free, paid, etc.)
5. **A sample or prototype** (if available)

### Terms:

- Permission is granted on a **project-by-project basis**
- A **royalty** may be negotiated depending on the scope and use
- You must **clearly credit** Fate's Edge and `Nicholas A. Gasper`
- You may **not** imply official endorsement or affiliation
- **Final approval** rests with the author

### Response Time:

Expect a response within **2–4 weeks**. If your project is time-sensitive, mention that in your request.

### What We're Looking For:

- Projects that **respect the tone and spirit** of Fate's Edge
- Work that is **well-written and professionally presented**
- Creators who are **open to collaboration and feedback**
- Content that **expands the world** rather than just copying it

## Part 4: Trademark and Logo Usage

Fate's Edge logos, titles, and branding are **trademarks** of `Nicholas A. Gasper`.

### For Open Content (SRD-only):

You may **not** use the Fate's Edge logo or title on your commercial or non-commercial work unless:

- Your work is clearly marked as "A Fate's Edge Supplement" or "Fate's Edge Compatible"
- You include a disclaimer: "This product is not affiliated with or endorsed by the creator of Fate's Edge."

### For Licensed Extended Content:

If you receive permission to use extended material, you **may** use the Fate's Edge logo, provided:

- You follow the logo usage guidelines provided with your license
- You include a "Licensed Product" tagline or disclaimer as agreed

## Part 5: Community Content (Free/Non-Commercial)

You are **always welcome** to create and share free content for Fate's Edge.

### You May (Without Permission):

- Write and share your own **adventures, NPCs, or settings** set in the Amaranthine
- Create **fan art, fiction, or actual play content** using Fate's Edge characters and locations
- Publish **free supplements, rules hacks, and house rules** using extended material
- Share your work on **community forums, blogs, social media, or itch.io** (free)

### You Must:

- Clearly credit Fate's Edge and `Nicholas A. Gasper`
- Include a disclaimer: "This is unofficial content for Fate's Edge and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the creator."
- **Not sell** content that uses extended material without permission

### We Love Seeing:

- New adventures set in the Amaranthine
- Character concepts and backgrounds
- House rules and system hacks
- Regional expansions and cultural explorations
- Actual play and fan fiction

Share your work! Tag us on social media (if you want to) and we'll help boost it.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I publish my Fate's Edge adventure on DriveThruRPG?**
- A: If it uses only SRD mechanics, yes. If it uses extended material (setting, characters, etc.), you need permission.

**Q: Can I run a Fate's Edge campaign on Twitch or YouTube?**
- A: Absolutely. No permission needed. We love seeing people play!

**Q: Can I sell Fate's Edge merchandise (T-shirts, dice, etc.)?**
- A: If it uses Fate's Edge logos, titles, or branding, you need permission. Generic "fantasy RPG" merchandise is fine.

**Q: Can I translate the SRD into another language?**
- A: Yes. The SRD is open for translation under the CC BY-NC-SA license. Translated versions must also be shared openly.

**Q: Can I translate extended material (the setting, adventures, etc.)?**
- A: Not without permission. Translations of extended material are derivative works and require explicit license.

**Q: What if I want to publish a commercial supplement that uses the SRD but references the Amaranthine?**
- A: If you reference the setting (e.g., "set in the city of Silkstrand"), that's extended material—you need permission. If you say "set in a fantasy city of canals and intrigue," that's fine—you're building your own world.
